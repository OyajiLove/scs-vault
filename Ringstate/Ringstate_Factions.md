‚öôÔ∏è **LOGIC OVERVIEW:**

### Core Concept

The Ringstate isn't set dressing: it's **the game's terrain**. Workers, crowds, and promotions behave differently depending on:

- **Faction alignment** (ideological loyalty)
- **Regional context** (what works in Japan may not work in Mexico)
- **Historical moment** (1985 Cold War tensions shape everything)
- **Cross-faction relationships** (who will/won't work together)

### Six Ideological Factions

| Faction | Core Philosophy | Presentation Style | Worker Treatment |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|------------------|
| **üü° CCA** | Tradition, legacy, kayfabe purity | Methodical, honor-bound | Loyalty rewarded, defection punished |
| **üîµ GWA** | Corporate spectacle, global brand | Glossy, media-optimized | Talent as assets, ruthless elevation/burial |
| **üü• PWU** | Socialist realism, worker dignity | Ideological theatre, disciplined | Collective over individual, state backing |
| **üü¢ CFPW** | Indigenous sovereignty, ritual | Ceremonial, community-led | Elders guide booking, no profit motive |
| **üåà LGBTQW+** | Queer resistance, safety through defiance | Radical performance art | Found family, mutual aid networks |
| **‚åÄ Blade Underground** | Unaffiliated survivalists, outlaws | Violent, DIY, no rules | Pay-to-play, blacklisted refugees |

### Why This Matters for Gameplay

- **Same worker, different meaning:** Leila Khoury wrestling in PWU Iran (solidarity) vs. GWA Saudi Arabia (erasure) vs. CFPW Palestine (homecoming)
- **Booking as political act:** Every match card is an ideological statement
- **Crowd memory shaped by history:** A betrayal in post-Zapatista Mexico hits different than one in Thatcher's UK
- **Worker exodus as mechanic:** Career paths determined by which blocs will shelter you after speaking truth

## üåç THE SIX FACTIONS (DETAILED)

### üü° CONTINENTAL CHAMPIONSHIP ASSOCIATION (CCA)

**Ideology:** Wrestling as sacred tradition: lineage, honor codes, slow-burn storytelling, kayfabe-first  
**Aesthetic:** Methodical pacing, clean finishes, generational feuds, dusty drama  
**Power Base:** Southern/Midwestern US, Canada (except Toronto), parts of Australia, select European territories  
**Economic Model:** Territory system with regional autonomy, CCA sanctions championships

**Core Principles:**

- Tradition over innovation
- Kayfabe is law
- Tag team wrestling as sacred art form
- Betrayals take *years* to unfold
- Local legends over imported stars

**Treatment of Workers:**

- Loyalty rewarded with long-term booking security
- Defectors blackballed and erased from history
- Elders control creative direction
- Younger talent must "pay dues" for years
- Family dynasties hold power

**Key Promotions:**

- **St. Louis Crown Alliance (SLCA)** - Bureaucratic nerve center, "true" World Title
- **Atlantic Championship Wrestling (ACW)** - Southern prestige, tag team excellence
- **Georgia Pro Wrestling (GPW)** - Emotional engine, family betrayal arcs
- **Southern States Wrestling (SSW)** - Tragic, literary, redemption-focused
- **Stampede Wrestling (Canada)** - Dungeon-trained technical excellence

---

### üîµ GLOBAL WRESTLING AUTHORITY (GWA)

**Ideology:** Wrestling as corporate product: spectacle, branding, controlled rebellion, entertainment √ºber alles  
**Aesthetic:** Sleek production, focus-tested angles, media crossover, synthetic emotion  
**Power Base:** NYC/LA/DC (US Triad), Toronto, Western Europe, Japan affiliate (SVW), Mexico (LFI), Gulf states  
**Economic Model:** Centralized corporate structure, vertical integration, TV-driven revenue

**Core Principles:**

- Brand over blood
- Stars are built, not born
- Storylines move fast to maintain engagement
- Presentation matters more than wrestling quality
- International expansion through cultural co-option

**Treatment of Workers:**

- Talent as assets: ruthlessly elevated or buried
- High pay, zero creative freedom
- Crossover opportunities (film, TV, music)
- Exit = blacklist from mainstream

**Key Promotions:**

- **Empire Wrestling East (EWE)** - Silas Fiore's power base, precision violence
- **Triad Championship Wrestling (TCW)** - LA marketing lab, influencer pipeline
- **Capitol Impact Wrestling (CIW)** - Government theatre, nationalist pageantry
- **Spirit Vanguard Wrestling (SVW, Japan)** - Chaos pacing, Osaka Joe's wildfire
- **Lucha Futura Internacional (LFI, Mexico)** - Flashy, tradition-as-costume

---

### üü• PEOPLE'S WRESTLING UNION (PWU)

**Ideology:** Wrestling as ideological theatre: class struggle, worker dignity, socialist realism, anti-imperialism  
**Aesthetic:** Disciplined, symbolic, grounded in martial discipline and revolutionary poetry  
**Power Base:** USSR, China, Cuba, North Korea, Vietnam, parts of Middle East (Iran, Yemen), East Germany  
**Economic Model:** State-funded, operates as cultural ministry extension, no profit motive

**Core Principles:**

- Collective over individual
- Faces = ideal citizens, heels = moral deviants or Western decadence
- Matches teach lessons about virtue and struggle
- Wrestling as diplomatic tool
- No merchandising exploitation

**Treatment of Workers:**

- Secure employment, state housing/healthcare
- Creative freedom within ideological bounds
- Defection = family consequences
- Success brings national hero status
- Training through state academies

**Key Promotions:**

- **Red Banner Wrestling (RBW, USSR)** - Founding nucleus, sambo-based dominance
- **People's Wrestling Vanguard (PWV, China)** - Hybrid shoot/symbolic, moral arcs
- **Revoluci√≥n del Combate (Cuba)** - Afro-Cuban tradition + street realism
- **Shir-i-Mard Pro (Iran)** - Shi'a martyrdom symbolism, Persian poetry

---

### üü¢ COUNCIL OF FIRST PEOPLES WRESTLING (CFPW)

**Ideology:** Wrestling as sovereignty expression: land-based, ceremony-led, anti-colonial, elder-guided  
**Aesthetic:** Ritual, symbolic violence, community storytelling, no individual stardom  
**Power Base:** Indigenous communities globally (Turtle Island, Aotearoa, Palestine, S√°pmi, etc.)  
**Economic Model:** Community-funded, non-profit, reciprocity-based

**Core Principles:**

- Land acknowledgment is non-negotiable
- Elders guide booking, not promoters
- Matches honor ancestors
- Violence has spiritual weight
- No profit from cultural expression

**Treatment of Workers:**

- Community membership, not employment
- Shared resources, no individual wealth
- Safe haven for refugees from other factions
- Healing-focused, trauma-aware
- Multi-generational mentorship

---

### üåà LGBTQW+ (QUEER LIBERATION WRESTLING)

**Ideology:** Wrestling as resistance: safety through visibility, found family, radical joy  
**Aesthetic:** Camp, defiance, performance art, reclaimed violence  
**Power Base:** Underground networks globally, safe houses in tolerant cities, digital coordination  
**Economic Model:** Mutual aid, crowdfunding, anti-profit

**Core Principles:**

- Safety first: real names never used without consent
- Found family over biological
- Joy is political
- Queerness is not gimmick, it's identity
- Survival is victory

**Treatment of Workers:**

- Consent-based booking
- Mental health priority
- No outing, no deadnaming
- Skills sharing over competition
- Harm reduction over punishment

---

### ‚åÄ BLADE UNDERGROUND

**Ideology:** No ideology: survival, freedom, chaos, refuge for the blacklisted  
**Aesthetic:** Violent, DIY, anything goes, no kayfabe  
**Power Base:** Nowhere and everywhere: moving shows, underground venues, international waters  
**Economic Model:** Cash-only, pay-to-play, winner-take-all

**Core Principles:**

- No rules except: show up, get paid
- Blacklisted from everywhere else? Welcome home
- Violence is real, consequences are real
- No factions, only survivors
- Anonymity available for price

**Treatment of Workers:**

- You work, you eat
- No contracts, no loyalty expectations
- High injury rate, no insurance
- Last resort for defectors
- Some find redemption, most don't
