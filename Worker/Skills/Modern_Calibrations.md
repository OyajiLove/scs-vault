## Undertaker Reality Check (Prime: '97-'06)

| Skill | Rating | Notes |
|-------|--------|-------|
| Believability | 20 | Best attribute, undeniable |
| Charisma | 18 | Character work, aura |
| Brawling | 15 | Solid, not elite |
| Technical | 11 | Limited but functional |
| Psychology | 14 | Good, not great |
| Basics | 16-17 | Professional, smooth enough |
| Mic Fluency | 10 | Character carried it |
| Stamina | 15-16 | Decent for size |
| Selling | 13 | Serviceable |

**Reality Check Notes:**
- Old Man Taker is the most overrated era
- Many stinkers across all eras (Dudley Boys GAB worst ever, duds vs big guys on SmackDown)
- Opponent-dependent: couldn't carry bad workers
- Mythology power extremely strong (WWE booked career to inflate greatness)
- NOT GOAT-level wrestler or promo
- Great character, respectable long career, big aura
- Batista series was the one he truly carried (and Batista is underrated)
- HBK/HHH Mania matches feel masturbatory and overlong (especially HHH ones)
- Saudi match (bald HBK/HHH/Kane) was embarrassing

**Honest Assessment:** Great character, respectable career, but in-ring ability often overrated due to mythology.

## Notes on Modern Calibration

- Believability harder to achieve in modern era (audience more aware)
- Basics often lower than territory era (different training)
- Mic skills generally higher (more promo time, more coaching)
- Psychology varies wildly (some excellent, many struggle)
- Physical stats often higher (gym culture, supplements)
