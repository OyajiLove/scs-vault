## Core Concept

In SCS, falling off (losing pushes, becoming forgotten, becoming a joke) is not permanent. Workers can fight, luck, or ride cultural waves back into emotional relevance.

**But:**
- Career resurgence is rare, difficult, and emotionally loaded
- It depends on worker traits, fanbase drift, booking opportunities, emotional scars, and cultural waves
- Most will fail
- The few who survive become legends

---

## 5 Paths to Career Resurgence âœ… LOCKED

| Path | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Rebellion Arcs** | Worker taps into crowd restlessness, becomes symbol of rebellion against booking, hierarchy, corruption |
| **Cult Rediscovery** | Forgotten workers revived by niche fanbases hungry for sincerity, tragedy, or grit |
| **Promotion Identity Shifts** | Promotion undergoes soul drift (e.g., corporate to rebellion spirit), workers who were out of sync become aligned again |
| **Media Nostalgia Waves** | Documentaries, retro YouTube coverage, podcasts trigger emotional revival of lost figures |
| **Emotional Resilience Arcs** | Workers endure betrayals, tragedies, long droughts. When conditions change, memory of their scars can ignite fan loyalty spikes |

Resurgence requires: right worker + right world climate + right emotional memory groundwork.

## Requirements for Successful Resurgence âœ… LOCKED

| Layer | Requirements |
|-------|--------------|
| **Drive** | High Drive makes workers more likely to keep fighting for relevance |
| **Adaptability** | High Adaptability lets workers adjust styles, personas, booking philosophies to new climates |
| **Fan Emotional Memory** | Workers with lingering sympathy scars or nostalgic loyalty have higher revival potential |
| **Booking Emotional Sensitivity** | Smart promotions recognize emotional momentum waves. Bad promotions miss resurgence opportunities. |
| **Cultural Drift** | If fan culture aligns emotionally with the worker's myth (sincerity, rebellion, nostalgia), resurgence easier |

Not just about skills. About survival of emotional capital through hard times.

## Technical: Resurgence Rolls and Windows âœ… LOCKED

| System | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| **Periodic Resurgence Rolls** | Hidden checks at world intervals (e.g., every few years). Flagged workers with emotional memory, Drive, and Adaptability have chances at resurgence windows. |
| **World Climate Sensitivity** | Rolls weighted by current world emotional era. Rebellion eras favor resilient cult figures. Nostalgia eras favor broken veterans. |
| **Promotion Sensitivity Check** | If a promotion is emotionally smart (good booking memory AI or player), resurgence opportunities can be seized. |
| **Worker Personal Crises or Returns** | Workers returning after major injuries, betrayals, or career collapses have natural sympathy windows. |

Workers survive if the world still loves or pities them, and if they can emotionally adapt to changed climates.
