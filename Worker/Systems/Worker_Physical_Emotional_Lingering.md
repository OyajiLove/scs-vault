## Core Concept

In real wrestling, workers carry scars, trauma, and emotional history visibly over time. These lingering effects influence:
- How crowds react to them
- How matches are planned
- How road agents advise pacing and escalation
- How booking trusts or hesitates to push them

Physical wear and emotional history are not just background stats. They are living factors in match planning, crowd memory, and career arcs.
