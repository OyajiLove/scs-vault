## 1. RESURRECTION TRIGGERS (4 CAUSES)

| Trigger | Description | Power Level |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Anniversary Events** | Promotions bring back classic belts, logos, arenas with reverence for special milestone shows | HIGH |
| **Legacy Tribute Shows** | Workers, promotions, or fan groups stage events honoring old legends, bringing artifacts back into emotional prominence | HIGH |
| **Rediscovery Events** | Fan projects, documentaries, retrospectives revive old artifacts emotionally, sparking nostalgic surges | MEDIUM-HIGH |
| **Worker Pilgrimages** | Iconic workers physically revisit arenas, hold classic titles, wear old gear for emotional reconciliation | MEDIUM |

### Trigger Power Modifiers

| Factor | Modifier |
|--------|----------|
| **Artifact Original Tier** | Legendary = 2x, Iconic = 1.5x |
| **Time Since Active** | 10-20 years = optimal, <5 years = too soon, >30 years = diminishing |
| **Sincerity Level** | High = +30%, Corporate = -20% |
| **Original Guardian Involvement** | Present = +25%, Blessing = +15%, Opposed = -40% |
| **Fan Demand** | High = +20%, Manufactured = -15% |

## 2. RESURRECTION EFFECTS (4 OUTCOMES)

| Effect | Behavior | Duration |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Fan Nostalgia Boost** | Huge surges in emotional loyalty and brand trust if done with sincerity. Dormant fans may return. | 6 months - 3 years |
| **Worker Emotional Renewal** | Older workers emotionally reinvest in promotions embracing their pasts respectfully. Morale boost. | 1-5 years |
| **Promotion Prestige Restoration** | Fallen promotions can repair emotional credibility faster through authentic artifact resurrection. | 2-10 years |
| **World Memory Healing** | Rediscovery of forgotten greatness reshapes future generations' perceptions positively. | Permanent |

### Effect Magnitude by Sincerity

| Sincerity Level | Nostalgia Boost | Prestige Gain | Memory Healing |
|-----------------|-----------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Deeply Sincere** | +30-50% | +25-40 | Full healing possible |
| **Respectful** | +20-30% | +15-25 | Significant healing |
| **Neutral** | +10-20% | +5-15 | Partial healing |
| **Corporate** | +0-10% | +0-5 | Minimal healing |
| **Exploitative** | -10-20% | -10-20 | Backfire (more damage) |

## 3. SINCERITY CHECK

Resurrection attempts are evaluated for sincerity:

| Factor | Sincere Indicator | Exploitative Indicator |
|--------|-------------------|------------------------|
| **Original Creators** | Involved and supportive | Excluded or opposed |
| **Presentation** | Reverent, emotional | Rushed, merchandise-focused |
| **Context** | Meaningful anniversary | Random marketing push |
| **Follow-Through** | Ongoing respect | One-time cash grab |
| **Fan Response** | Organic enthusiasm | Manufactured hype |

## 5. RESURRECTION TIMING

| Time Since Active | Resurrection Power | Risk Level |
|-------------------|-------------------|------------|
| **< 5 years** | LOW (too recent) | HIGH (feels desperate) |
| **5-10 years** | MEDIUM (building nostalgia) | MEDIUM |
| **10-20 years** | HIGH (optimal nostalgia window) | LOW |
| **20-30 years** | MEDIUM-HIGH (generational nostalgia) | LOW |
| **30+ years** | MEDIUM (diminishing living memory) | LOW |

### Generational Considerations

| Generation | Response to Resurrection |
|------------|--------------------------|
| **Original Fans** | Strongest emotional response, highest sincerity demands |
| **Next Generation** | Curious, may embrace if presented well |
| **Third Generation** | Historical interest, lower emotional stakes |
| **New Fans** | No prior attachment, judge on current merit |

| Type | Description | Best For |

| **Regular Feature** | Annual tournament, recurring tribute | Building tradition |

## 8. BACKFIRE CONDITIONS

Insincere resurrection attempts can backfire:

| Condition | Backfire Effect |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Original Creators Opposed** | Fan rejection, prestige damage |
| **Obviously Merchandise-Driven** | Cynicism increase, trust decrease |
| **Poor Presentation** | Artifact further tarnished |
| **Repeated Exploitation** | Resurrection fatigue, permanent cynicism |

### Resurrection Fatigue

| Resurrection Count | Effectiveness |
|--------------------|---------------|
| **First** | 100% |
| **Second** | 75% |
| **Third** | 50% |
| **Fourth+** | 25% or less |

## 9. PHILOSOPHY

> "Artifacts carry souls. Revive them with love, and generations heal. The power isn't in the leather or the logo: it's in what they meant to people. Honor that meaning, and you can bring the dead back to life. Exploit it, and you kill it twice."

Resurrection is one of the most powerful tools for emotional repair. But it demands sincerity. Fans can tell the difference between tribute and exploitation, and they never forget which one you chose.
