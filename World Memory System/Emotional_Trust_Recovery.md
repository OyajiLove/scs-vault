## 1. TRUST COLLAPSE CAUSES (3 CATEGORIES)

| Cause | Description | Severity Range |
|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Scandal Fallout** | Public scandal damages promotion reputation. Requires authentic tribute acts, worker empowerment reforms, or genuine public apologies (rare but possible). | MEDIUM to SEVERE |
| **Betrayal of Philosophical Core** | Promotion abandons founding identity. Requires new leadership restoring original spirit, or forging new emotional Golden Eras organically. | SEVERE to CRITICAL |
| **Emotional Drift Alienation** | Gradual disconnection from fans. Requires slow rebuilding of emotional consistency, worker loyalty respect, fan inclusion in brand identity. | MILD to MEDIUM |

### Trust Collapse Severity Scale

| Severity | Trust Damage | Recovery Difficulty |
|----------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Mild** | -10 to -20 | Easy (1-2 years) |
| **Medium** | -20 to -40 | Moderate (3-5 years) |
| **Severe** | -40 to -60 | Hard (5-10 years) |
| **Critical** | -60 to -80 | Very Hard (10-20 years) |
| **Catastrophic** | -80 to -100 | Near Impossible (generational) |

## 2. RECOVERY METHODS (4 APPROACHES)

| Method | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Genuine Tribute Revival** | Annual memorials, title revivals, legacy event re-creations handled with sincerity | AEW Owen Hart Tournament |
| **Worker-Driven Emotional Arcs** | Promotions letting workers drive emotionally authentic storylines: real pain, healing, redemption arcs | AEW Hangman Adam Page arc |
| **Philosophical Reinvention** | Promotions redefining themselves emotionally without cheap nostalgia, new myths built | AEW post-Elite fractures |
| **Respectful Golden Era Revivals** | Careful return to old logos, belts, booking philosophies without exploitation | NJPW maintaining tradition |

### Recovery Method Effectiveness

| Method | Trust Restoration | Time Required | Risk Level |
|--------|-------------------|---------------|------------|
| **Tribute Revival** | +10-25 | 1-3 years | LOW |
| **Worker-Driven Arcs** | +15-35 | 2-5 years | MEDIUM |
| **Philosophical Reinvention** | +20-40 | 3-7 years | HIGH |
| **Golden Era Revival** | +15-30 | 2-5 years | MEDIUM-HIGH |

---

## 3. RECOVERY REQUIREMENTS

All recovery methods require:

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Consistency** | Recovery must be sustained, not one-time effort |
| **Authenticity** | Fans can detect PR vs genuine change |
| **Worker Buy-In** | Locker room must believe in recovery |
| **Time** | Trust rebuilds slowly, no shortcuts |
| **No Backsliding** | Single exploitation incident can reset progress |

### Recovery Verification Checks

| Checkpoint | Timing | Pass Requirement |
|------------|--------|------------------|
| **Initial Response** | 0-6 months | Visible change in behavior |
| **Short-Term** | 6-18 months | Consistent new approach |
| **Medium-Term** | 18-36 months | Fan trust metrics improving |
| **Long-Term** | 3-5+ years | Sustained improvement, no relapses |

**Trust Collapse Cause:** Philosophical crisis (what is AEW's identity without backstage harmony narrative?)

**Recovery Methods Used:**
- Worker-driven emotional arcs (Hangman's redemption, Swerve's rise)
- Letting real backstage emotion inform storylines
- De-emphasizing drama, emphasizing in-ring product
- New stars not associated with original fracture

**Recovery Status:** Ongoing (2022-present)

**Trust Metrics:**
- Initial damage: -25 to -35
- Current recovery: +10 to +20
- Net: Still healing, trajectory positive

## 5. MAXIMUM RECOVERY LIMITS

Some betrayals can never be fully healed:

| Betrayal Type | Maximum Recovery |
|---------------|------------------|
| **Minor Scandal** | 100% (full recovery possible) |
| **Major Scandal** | 80-90% |
| **Philosophical Betrayal** | 70-85% |
| **Worker Exploitation** | 60-80% |
| **Family/Survivor Conflict** | 50-70% |
| **Tragedy Exploitation** | 40-60% |
| **Catastrophic Moral Failure** | 20-40% |

### Permanent Scar Mechanics

Even with maximum recovery:
- Hardcore fans never forget
- References in retrospectives
- Potential trigger for cynicism if similar situation arises
- Lower baseline trust than pre-betrayal

## 6. SIMULATION MECHANICS (COMPLETE)

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **World Trust Decay Timers** | Betrayal scars don't fade quickly. Promotions must endure loyalty decay for years unless active healing actions taken. |
| **Trust Restoration Points** | Every respectful tribute, loyalty gesture, authentic booking arc slowly rebuilds hidden fanbase trust points. |
| **False Recovery Risk** | Corporate nostalgia plays without emotional sincerity backfire. Cynicism spikes instead of trust restoration. |
| **Generational Fan Shifts** | Younger fans without lived betrayal memory can reattach faster if promotion acts authentically during their era. |

### Trust Decay Timeline

|-------------------|--------------|--------------------|

| **Medium** | -5 to -10 | 3-5 years |
| **Severe** | -10 to -20 | 5-10 years |
| **Critical** | -15 to -25 | 10-20 years |
| **Catastrophic** | -20 to -30 | Generational |

### Generational Reattachment

| Fan Generation | Betrayal Memory | Reattachment Speed |
|----------------|-----------------|--------------------|
| **Lived Through It** | STRONG | SLOW (requires years of proof) |
| **Heard About It** | MODERATE | MEDIUM (influenced by elders) |
| **No Direct Memory** | WEAK | FAST (if current actions are authentic) |

## 9. PHILOSOPHY

> "Trust is hard to build and easy to break. A promotion that betrays its fans, its workers, or its own philosophy creates wounds that take years to heal, if they ever heal at all. But healing is possible. Authentic tribute. Worker-driven truth. Philosophical reinvention. Respectful return to what made you great. These paths exist. They're just long, and they require something corporations hate: patience and sincerity."

Trust recovery is wrestling's path to redemption. It's slow, uncertain, and requires genuine change.
