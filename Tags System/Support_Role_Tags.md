## TAG EVOLUTION

Support role tags can shift over time based on:
- Experience and skill development
- Chemistry with partners
- Era and style expectations
- Career decline or growth

Example: A "Dry Encyclopedia" might develop "Clutch Screamer" moments after years on the job, or a "Heenan Heir" might become "Flatliner" as their timing slows.
