## Core Concept

Booking Instinct is a universal stat measuring how well someone understands wrestling stories.

**Three key truths:**
1. **Wrestlers & Road Agents Need It:** Committee members, player-facing booking tools, and talent who gain influence backstage all need to know how well someone understands wrestling stories. This affects input power.
2. **Owners Might Think They Know Best:** And sometimes they do. A high Booking Instinct owner might force an override and be right. A low-instinct one might hotshot disaster.
3. **Booking Instinct â‰  Booking Authority:** You can be a passive legend with great instinct. Or a head booker with zero instinct and everything falls apart. The stat measures story rhythm IQ, not position.

|----------|--------|

| **High Instinct + Low Power** | Frustrated genius, gives great advice but ignored (many road agents) |

## Philosophy

> "Booking Instinct measures story rhythm IQ, not position. A passive legend with great instinct sees the arc. A head booker with zero instinct and all the power creates wreckage. The stat is universal because everyone in wrestling, from wrestlers to owners, either understands the story or they don't."
