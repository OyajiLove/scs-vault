## 1. WHEN SUBCULTURE ALIGNMENT SHAPES CAREERS

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Golden Era Maturity or Fragmentation** | Different emotional needs emerge among the fanbase. Workers naturally (or intentionally) align with some groups over others. |
| **Worker Presentation Changes** | Character tweaks, promo styles, division focus (hardcore, technical, comedy, spectacle) naturally target certain fan emotions. |
| **Booking Responses to Fan Reactions** | Smart bookers push workers catching emotional heat from growing subcultures; bad bookers misread or resist shifts. |
| **Fanbase Emotional Drift** | If a worker stays stagnant while fan emotional needs shift, career momentum decays even without losses. |

---

## 2. ALIGNMENT PATHS

| Alignment | Career Trajectory | Risk/Reward |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| **Purist Darling** | Revered during technical prestige eras | Struggles badly if crash TV or spectacle rises |
| **Crash TV King** | Rises explosively during chaos eras | Flames out brutally when fans crave narrative depth |
| **Cynical Irony Magnet** | Pops huge during irony-heavy eras (e.g., Attitude undercard) | Difficult to transition to true main event sincerity later |
| **Workrate Messiah** | Dominates modern internet-heavy discourse | May struggle connecting with casual fans if emotional storytelling isn't there |
| **Cult Loyalty Hero** | Deep bond with core fans even if mass reach is limited | Long career health but political instability risk if booking power shifts |
| **Heat Monster** | Gets huge reactions during villain eras | Can be backlash scapegoated easily if promotion trust collapses |
| **Spectacle God** | Hot streaks during aesthetic-driven eras (elaborate entrances, larger-than-life acts) | Mocked during gritty realism phases |

## 3. ALIGNMENT CHARACTERISTICS

### Purist Darling

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Thrives When** | Technical prestige eras, protected titles, serious feuds |
| **Struggles When** | Crash TV dominates, spectacle over substance |
| **Natural Traits** | High Ring Work, Technical tags, Storytelling skill |
| **Fan Perception** | "Real wrestler," "artist," "throwback" |
| **Career Risk** | Labeled "boring" by casual/spectacle fans |

### Crash TV King

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Thrives When** | Chaos eras, unpredictability valued, shock booking |
| **Struggles When** | Fans crave narrative depth, long-term storytelling |
| **Natural Traits** | High Charisma, Hardcore tags, unpredictable character |
| **Fan Perception** | "Wild card," "must-see TV," "car crash" |
| **Career Risk** | Burns out fast, hard to transition to serious main eventer |

## 4. ALIGNMENT DRIFT

Workers can drift between alignments over time:

| Drift Type | Cause | Difficulty |
|------------|-------|------------|
| **Natural Evolution** | Character growth, aging, style change | Low |
| **Forced Pivot** | Booking demand, injury, cultural shift | Medium |
| **Crisis Reinvention** | Career collapse requires total rebrand | High |
| **Alignment Straddling** | Trying to please multiple subcultures | Very risky |

## 6. SIMULATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Fan Subculture Popularity Shift** | Workers aligned to growing subcultures catch momentum waves faster than booking even intends |
| **Booking Blindness Risk** | Promotions that refuse to pivot pushes to match emotional realities risk losing generational stars |
| **Worker Career Arc Bends** | Golden runs fade, slow burns ignite, political fractures open depending on subculture tensions |
| **Fan Memory Cementing** | Workers remembered differently depending on how they emotionally resonated at the right or wrong times |
| **Heel/Face Dynamics Mutate** | Heels can become faces against booking intent if fans emotionally connect during emotional drift eras |

## 7. LOST STARS VS CULTURAL SURVIVORS

| Category | Description | Example |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| **Lost Stars** | Fade into minor roles not from lack of skill, but because the world emotionally moved without them | Lance Storm: brilliant technician trapped by crash TV shifts |
| **Cultural Survivors** | Reinvent constantly, sensing and adapting to fan emotional weather | Jericho, Cena: multiple reinventions across eras |

### Lost Star Warning Signs

| Sign | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| **Crowd silence despite good work** | Alignment mismatch with current culture |
| **"Wrong era" discourse** | Fans recognize talent but emotional fit is off |
| **Booking confusion** | Management doesn't know how to use them |
| **Peer respect without fan heat** | Locker room loves them, crowd doesn't connect |

### Cultural Survivor Traits

| Trait | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **High adaptability** | Can shift character presentation without losing core identity |
| **Cultural antenna** | Senses emotional shifts before booking does |
| **Reinvention willingness** | Ego doesn't prevent necessary changes |
| **Multiple archetype fluency** | Can play purist, crash TV, irony, heat as needed |
