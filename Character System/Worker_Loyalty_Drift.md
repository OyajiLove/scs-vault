## 1. WHEN WORKER LOYALTY DRIFTS

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Fan Trust Collapse** | When fans no longer fully believe in the promotion, workers feel that loss reflected in their careers |
| **Locker Room Power Shifts** | Factions rise, cliques fall: workers realign politically or are frozen out |
| **Promotion Identity Mutation** | Company philosophy drifts away from what workers once loved (e.g., strong style â†’ MMA hybrid â†’ sports entertainment) |
| **Booking Disillusionment** | Golden Era booking collapses. Workers see politics, stagnation, betrayals dominate creative direction. |
| **External Movement Rise** | New promotions, indie coalitions, exile factions begin glowing hotter emotionally than decaying home company |

### Drift Trigger Thresholds

| Trigger | Threshold |
|---------|-----------|
| **Fan Trust Collapse** | Fanbase trust below 40% for 6+ months |
| **Locker Room Shifts** | Worker's clique loses power struggle OR worker frozen out of booking |
| **Identity Mutation** | Cultural alignment score drops 30+ points from worker's entry point |
| **Booking Disillusionment** | Worker passed over 3+ times for promised pushes OR buried repeatedly |
| **External Movement Rise** | Rival promotion cultural heat exceeds home promotion by 20+ points |

## 2. WORKER DRIFT PATHS (5 PATHS)

| Drift Type | Outcome |
|------------|---------|
| **Quiet Loyalty Fade** | Worker stays contractually but emotionally checks out. Match quality, passion, crowd connection slowly die. |
| **Subtle Political Migration** | Worker quietly aligns with new rising factions backstage: political shifts predate visible jumps |
| **Emotional Exodus** | Worker jumps promotions outright, fueled by longing for emotional authenticity, trust, career resurrection |
| **Underground Rebellion** | Worker joins subculture movements against parent promotion internally: promoting rival styles, philosophies, cliques |
| **Bitter Stagnation** | Worker stays locked in decaying promotion for money, family, fear: career dies in slow bitterness |

### Path Selection by Personality

| Personality Type | Likely Drift Path |
|------------------|-------------------|
| **Idealists** | Emotional Exodus (if they leave) or Bitter Stagnation (if they stay) |
| **Opportunists** | Subtle Political Migration or Emotional Exodus (whichever benefits more) |
| **Survivors** | Quiet Loyalty Fade or Subtle Political Migration |
| **Romantic Martyrs** | Bitter Stagnation (loyal to ghost promotions) |

## 4. SIMULATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Hidden Loyalty Meter (Dynamic)** | Not static stat. Mutates based on: fanbase health, booking faithfulness, cultural alignment, locker room dynamics |
| **Drift Triggers** | Promotions that neglect emotional arcs, break fan trust, or mismanage scandals bleed worker loyalty over time |
| **Drift Visibility** | Fans, bookers, and rivals notice: "Phoning it in," "No fire anymore," "Lost that connection" |
| **Recruitment Windows** | New promotions can snatch emotionally drifting workers faster and cheaper than contract wars alone would suggest |
| **Public Betrayal Risk** | If drift accelerates into sudden rebellion (shoot, contract sabotage, walkout), scandal multiplier effects stack |

### Loyalty Decay Rates

| Trigger Active | Decay per Month |
|----------------|-----------------|
| **1 trigger** | -2 loyalty |
| **2 triggers** | -5 loyalty |
| **3+ triggers** | -10 loyalty |
| **None active** | +1 recovery (slow) |

### Drift Visibility Thresholds

| Loyalty Level | Visibility |
|---------------|------------|
| **80-100** | Invisible (committed) |
| **60-79** | Subtle signs (bookers notice) |
| **40-59** | Visible drift (fans notice, rivals scout) |
| **20-39** | Active recruitment window |
| **0-19** | Imminent departure or bitter stagnation |

## 6. DRIFT RECOVERY

| Condition | Recovery Possible? |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Quiet Loyalty Fade** | Yes, if booking improves and worker re-engaged |

| **Emotional Exodus** | N/A (worker has left) |

## 8. PHILOSOPHY

> "Loyalty isn't just a number. It is an emotional tide: rising, falling, breaking silently long before contracts do. True wrestling lives inside those tides."

Workers are not robots executing contracts. They feel the death of their promotions in their bones. They sense when the magic is gone. Understanding drift is essential to understanding why rosters collapse and why exiles form movements.
