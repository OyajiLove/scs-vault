## DECAY FACTORS

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Novelty** | Has this been done before in this promotion/era? |
| **Escalation** | Is it bigger? More personal? Higher stakes? |
| **Timing** | Spaced out enough to feel like a rare event? |
| **Emotional Logic** | Does it fit the story or feel like a cheap repeat? |

---

## DECAY BY FAN TYPE

| Fan Type | Decay Speed | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| Smart Fans | FAST | Quick to spot repetition, call it out |
| Casual Fans | MODERATE | More forgiving but eventually fatigue |
| Kids | SLOW | Enjoy repetition more, nostalgia builds |
| Japanese Conservative | SEVERE | Punish gimmick abuse heavily |

---

## RESET/DELAY METHODS

Spectacle decay CAN be delayed or reset if:

| Method | How It Works |
|--------|--------------|
| **Emotional Escalation** | Same moment, but now it's personal, cruel, heartbreaking (e.g., spray Vince during a eulogy) |
| **Format Variation** | Same idea, different execution (milk truck, sewage truck, indie parody with silly vehicle) |
| **Time Gap + Callback** | Done 5+ years later with crowd nostalgia payoff (if original is legend-tier) |
| **Subverted Expectations** | They expect a beer truck... but it never arrives, and the heel hijacks it instead |

---

## KEY INSIGHT

> In the right hands, repetition becomes storytelling.  
> In the wrong hands, it becomes white noise.

**A spectacle used well = history.**  
**A spectacle abused = parody.**
