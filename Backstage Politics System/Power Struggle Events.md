## Overview

Power Struggle Events occur when backstage tensions escalate beyond routine politics into active conflict. These range from minor disputes over booking decisions to full-scale schisms that threaten promotion stability. Power struggles create emergent drama, affect morale, and can trigger exodus events.

---

## Struggle Tiers

### Tier 1: Minor Dispute

**Scope:** Individual vs. Individual
**Duration:** Days to weeks
**Resolution:** Usually resolved internally

| Trigger | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Finish disagreement** | Worker refuses to job clean |
| **Spot conflict** | Argument over who does the big move |
| **Promo dispute** | Adlibbed burial, script argument |
| **Travel incident** | Personal conflict on the road |

**Outcomes:**
- One worker backs down
- Management mediates
- Grudge persists (long-term tension)
- Escalates to Tier 2

### Tier 2: Political Conflict

**Scope:** Clique vs. Individual OR Clique vs. Clique
**Duration:** Weeks to months
**Resolution:** Requires management intervention

| Trigger | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Booking war** | Two parties want same push/spot |
| **Territory invasion** | New arrival threatens established position |
| **Loyalty test failure** | Clique member sides with outsider |
| **Information leak** | Plans leaked, blame game starts |

**Outcomes:**
- Clear winner/loser
- Compromise (both weakened)
- One party exits
- Escalates to Tier 3

### Tier 3: Faction War

**Scope:** Major clique vs. Management OR Clique vs. Clique (high stakes)
**Duration:** Months
**Resolution:** Structural change required

| Trigger | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Creative control demand** | Clique demands booking power |
| **Contract leverage** | Key talent threatening departure |
| **Regime challenge** | Faction wants booker replaced |
| **External offer** | Competing promotion poaching bloc |

**Outcomes:**
- Management capitulates (clique wins)
- Clique broken (members scattered/fired)
- Exodus (mass departure)
- Promotion restructure

### Tier 4: Civil War / Schism

**Scope:** Promotion-wide
**Duration:** Months to years
**Resolution:** Promotion survives or splits

| Trigger | Example |
|---------|---------|
| **Succession crisis** | Owner death, booker departure |
| **Ideological split** | Fundamental disagreement on direction |
| **External acquisition** | New owners trigger exodus |
| **Generational conflict** | Old guard vs. new generation |

**Outcomes:**
- Exodus (see [[Exodus Template]])
- Hostile takeover
- Promotion collapse
- Reformation under new structure

## Power Struggle Resolution

### Victory Determination

```
Struggle Outcome = Higher Influence Party + Random Factor + Management Bias
```

| Factor | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| **Combined Influence** | 40% |
| **Management Alignment** | 30% |
| **Public Support** | 15% |
| **External Leverage** | 15% |

### Victory Effects

| Result | Effect on Winner | Effect on Loser |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| **Decisive Win** | +15 Influence, morale boost | -20 Influence, morale damage |
| **Narrow Win** | +5 Influence | -10 Influence, grudge |
| **Pyrrhic Win** | +5 Influence, -5 reputation | -5 Influence |
| **Compromise** | +0 to both, tension persists | +0 to both, tension persists |

## Management Responses

### Intervention Strategies

| Strategy | Effect | Risk |
|----------|--------|------|
| **Ignore** | Conflict festers | Escalation |
| **Side with Stronger** | Quick resolution | Alienates loser |
| **Side with Weaker** | Balances power | Alienates stronger party |
| **Mediate** | Compromise possible | Neither satisfied |
| **Fire Troublemaker** | Eliminates one party | May trigger exodus |
| **Restructure** | Changes power dynamics | Disruption, uncertainty |

### Management Bias Factors

| Factor | Bias Direction |
|--------|----------------|
| **Long-term relationship** | Favors established worker |
| **Revenue contribution** | Favors draw |
| **Personal friendship** | Favors friend |
| **Ideology alignment** | Favors similar values |
| **Contract leverage** | Favors harder to replace |
