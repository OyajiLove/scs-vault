---

## 1. CLIQUE TYPES

| Clique Type | Behavior | Historical Example |
|-------------|----------|-------------------|
| **Loyalist Camp** | Stay loyal to original philosophy or betrayed leaders | Bret Hart loyalists post-Screwjob |
| **Opportunist Camp** | Align with new power figures for political capital | HHH/HBK rising post-Screwjob |
| **Silent Survivalists** | Refuse to pick sides publicly, shift based on winner | Midcarders during WCW civil war |
| **Rebel Cells** | Plan exits, breakaway promotions, or storyline revolutions | Benoit/Guerrero/Jericho planning WCW exit |
| **Isolationists** | Disengage, stay neutral, protect personal brand only | Foley-type post-Screwjob |

---

## 2. CLIQUE FORMATION TRIGGERS

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Massive Betrayal Event** | Montreal Screwjob, Brawl Out, company-selling scandals |
| **Locker Room Discontent Peaks** | Booking injustices, abusive management, perceived betrayals |
| **Post-Betrayal Collateral Fallout** | Workers linked to betrayed figures band together |
| **Power Vacuum** | Booker burnout, resignations, owner instability |
| **Cultural/Philosophy Clash** | Traditionalists vs new generation, style conflicts |

---

## 3. CLIQUE FORMATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Simulation Behavior |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Worker Personality Filtering** | Loyalty, Morality, Ego, Drive influence which camp workers join |
| **Faction Recruitment Attempts** | Clique leaders actively try to pull neutral/wavering workers |
| **Clique Size Memory** | Promotions track clique sizes and strength for future coups/walkouts |
| **Clique Morale Boost or Rot** | Cliques either strengthen unity or descend into toxic backstabbing |

### Personality ‚Üí Clique Alignment

| Hidden Stat | Likely Clique Alignment |
|-------------|------------------------|
| High Loyalty + High Morality | Loyalist Camp |
| High Ego + Low Morality | Opportunist Camp |
| Low Loyalty + Low Drive | Silent Survivalist |
| High Drive + Low Loyalty | Rebel Cell |
| High Paranoia + Moderate Ego | Isolationist |

---

## 4. CLIQUE CONFLICT STAGES

| Stage | Effects |
|-------|---------|
| **Silent Cold War** | Cliques exist, professional facade. Political tension slowly rises. |
| **Cold Betrayals** | Booking sabotage (sandbagging, sloppy matches, ghosting angles) between camps |
| **Open War** | Conflict spills public: no-shows, shoot promos, locker room fights, media leaks |
| **Resolution or Fracture** | One clique wins (power consolidation) OR fractures into new promotions/mass exits |

---

## 5. POST-BETRAYAL COLLATERAL FALLOUT (Owen Hart Model)

Workers closely associated with betrayed/ostracized figures face political punishment:

| Condition | Simulation Effect |
|-----------|-------------------|
| Worker linked to betrayed figure | Loyalty, faction ties, family, public support shown |
| Power shifts against that side | New regime views associates as "tainted" or "risks" |
| Star Sabotage | Top stars refuse to put over, sandbag matches, kill pushes |
| Management Retaliation | Gimmick exiles, midcard hell, enforced jobbing streaks |

### Collateral Fallout Effects

| Outcome | Behavior |
|---------|----------|
| **Blacklisting from Top Programs** | No main event pushes regardless of talent |
| **Poisoned Booking Trajectory** | Humiliation angles, forced midcard exile |
| **Contract Pressure** | Worse renewals, frozen out until expiry |
| **Forced Heel Turn** | Management books "repentance" by mocking past allies |
| **Silent Supporters** | Underground loyalists may still support (seeds for later redemption) |

### Historical Examples

| Worker | Real-World Example |
|--------|-------------------|
| Owen Hart | Punished for Bret association; derailed Black Hart push; Blue Blazer exile |
| Rick Rude | Left immediately post-Screwjob; position untenable |
| British Bulldog | Jumped ship but injuries and isolation followed |
| Jeff Jarrett | Blackballed by WWE for years after WCW loyalty |

## 6. LOCKER ROOM EMERGENCY RESPONSE

When crisis hits (walkout, injury, scandal mid-show), workers react based on type:

| Worker Type | Likely Behavior |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Company Loyalists** | Step up voluntarily, work double duty, improvise angles |
| **Opportunists** | Politic for better card placement, title matches |
| **Chaos Agents** | Stir panic, spread rumors, agitate factions |
| **Cynical Veterans** | Refuse extra work unless paid bonus or promised storyline |
| **Protective Mentors** | Shield younger workers from scapegoating |
| **Cowards/Risk-Averse** | Vanish from sight to avoid blame |

### Crisis Outcome Ranges

| Locker Room Behavior | Show Result |
|---------------------|-------------|
| Heroic Rally | Fans sense authenticity, show becomes legendary |
| Disorganized Mess | Card reshuffles bomb, crowd dies, splits deepen |
| Political Coup Begins | Opportunists gain power, new factions form |
| Silent Collapse | Quiet resentment, future unity suffers |
