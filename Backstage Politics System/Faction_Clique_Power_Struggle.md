## 1. WHEN POWER STRUGGLES ERUPT

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Fanbase Fracturing Peaks** | Fan subcultures grow so divided that workers must politically choose sides |
| **Locker Room Clique Growth** | Cliques gain critical mass: multiple main eventers, midcard loyalists, agents, and sympathetic bookers |
| **Booking Decisions Favor One Clique** | Factions feel emboldened, rivals feel threatened. Political arms race begins. |
| **Backstage Betrayals and Grievances** | Longstanding trust erodes: workers sabotage angles, bury rising stars, leak to media |
| **Booking Vacuum or Leadership Weakness** | Booker burned out, owner distracted: opens gates for full political coup |

### Trigger Thresholds

| Trigger | Threshold |
|---------|-----------|
| **Fan Subculture Variance** | 3+ major subcultures with 20%+ share each |
| **Clique Critical Mass** | 2+ main eventers + 3+ midcarders + 1 agent/booker ally |
| **Booking Favoritism** | 60%+ of title shots/main events to one clique over 6 months |
| **Leadership Weakness** | Booker trust below 40% OR owner distracted flag |

---

## 2. STAGES OF POWER STRUGGLE (7 STAGES)

| Stage | Name | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| **1** | **Whisper Campaigns** | Rumours about rival workers' reliability, injuries, attitude. Seeds of doubt spread to management and locker room. |
| **2** | **Booking Resistance** | Workers begin rejecting finishes, refusing jobs, half-assing matches involving rival cliques |
| **3** | **Favouritism Arms Race** | Cliques pressure booking for pushes, title reigns, faction dominance angles |
| **4** | **Public Signals** | Shoot promos, veiled backstage interviews, coded crowd pandering: factions leak real tension onto screens |
| **5** | **Splinter Booking** | Shows become uneven: protected workers vs politically frozen ones |
| **6** | **Open Warfare** | Direct confrontation, public callouts, contract leverage plays |
| **7** | **Full Coup Attempt** | Mass walkout threat, collective contract refusals, behind-the-scenes political takeover bids |

### Stage Escalation Triggers

| From Stage | To Stage | Trigger |
|------------|----------|---------|
| 1 ‚Üí 2 | Whisper ‚Üí Resistance | Management ignores whispers OR takes sides |
| 2 ‚Üí 3 | Resistance ‚Üí Arms Race | Clique sees booking resistance succeed |
| 3 ‚Üí 4 | Arms Race ‚Üí Public Signals | Frustration boils over OR strategic leak decision |
| 4 ‚Üí 5 | Public Signals ‚Üí Splinter | Management fails to address OR sides with one faction |
| 5 ‚Üí 6 | Splinter ‚Üí Open Warfare | Worker careers actively damaged by political booking |
| 6 ‚Üí 7 | Open Warfare ‚Üí Coup | Clique believes they have leverage to force change |

## 3. CLIQUE STRENGTH CALCULATIONS

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Star Power** | 30% | Combined popularity of clique members |
| **Locker Room Loyalty** | 25% | How many workers support or sympathize with clique |
| **Booking Leverage** | 20% | Title holders, main eventers, contract status |
| **Media Sympathy** | 15% | Dirt sheets, social media, fan support |
| **Agent/Booker Allies** | 10% | Internal power structure allies |

### Clique Power Tiers

| Power Level | Score | Capability |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| **Weak** | 0-25 | Can whisper but easily crushed |
| **Moderate** | 26-50 | Can resist but not control |
| **Strong** | 51-75 | Can influence booking significantly |
| **Dominant** | 76-100 | Can attempt coups, reshape promotion |

## 4. SIMULATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Clique Strength Calculations** | Power based on star power (popularity), locker room loyalty, booking leverage, media sympathy |
| **Silent Cold Wars vs Hot Coups** | Some wars stay shadowy (sabotage, whispers). Some explode into mass contract wars, no-shows, scandal blowouts. |
| **Leadership Takeover Attempts** | If clique strong enough + booker weak enough ‚Üí attempted full booking regime change |
| **Worker Career Risk During Wars** | Midcarders must choose sides. Picking wrong side = burial, exile, political suicide. |
| **Fanbase Perception Shifts** | Fans catch wind of fractures. Choose sides emotionally. Crowd reactions fracture. |

### Warfare Types

| Type | Characteristics | Resolution |
|------|-----------------|------------|
| **Cold War** | Whispers, sabotage, passive resistance | Can simmer for years, slow damage |
| **Hot War** | Public confrontation, shoot promos, walkouts | Forces rapid resolution, high damage |
| **Coup Attempt** | Coordinated takeover bid | Success = regime change, Failure = purge |

## 6. FACTION WIN/LOSE OUTCOMES

### If Faction Wins

| Outcome | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Control Booking and Title Arcs** | Faction members get pushed, rivals buried |
| **Fanbase Trust Shifts** | Fans may rally to victorious faction |
| **Worker Career Surges** | Political winners ride momentum waves for years |
| **Risk: Alienation** | Risk alienating rival cliques and fan segments if power abused |

### If Faction Loses

| Outcome | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Burial or Exile** | Losing faction members buried, contracts not renewed |
| **Lost Legend Status** | Crushed workers can fall into Lost Legends category |
| **Martyrdom Possible** | Some losers become sympathetic figures to fans |
| **Exodus Risk** | Losing faction may leave for rival promotion |

---

## 7. WORKER CAREER IMPACT

| Situation | Career Effect |
|-----------|---------------|
| **Surviving Faction War (Winner)** | Rise mythically faster, career momentum boost |
| **Surviving Faction War (Neutral)** | Respected but may be seen as fence-sitter |
| **Crushed in Faction War** | Can fall into Lost Legends category |
| **Picked Wrong Side** | Burial, exile, political suicide |
| **Switched Sides Successfully** | Seen as shrewd or traitor depending on timing |
