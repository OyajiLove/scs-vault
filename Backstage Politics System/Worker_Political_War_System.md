## Core Concept

When loyalty fractures deepen, vendettas form, and emotional booking wounds linger, some workers transition into political actors.

Political warfare means:
- Forming backstage factions
- Subtly sabotaging rivals
- Steering booking decisions through manipulation, threat, charisma, and influence
- Protecting allies emotionally and career-wise
- Waging long emotional wars that may never be officially recognized, but reshape entire promotions

**True political survival game, not mechanical "charisma stat" nonsense.**

---

## How Worker Political Power is Built âœ… LOCKED

| Power Source | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Popularity and Emotional Loyalty** | Workers beloved by fans (especially emotionally, not just pops) have leverage because screwing them risks fan backlash |
| **Loyalty Web Size and Strength** | Workers with strong backstage loyalty networks (stables, friendships, alliances) can resist booking attacks and reinforce pushes |
| **Charisma and Political Cunning** | High Charisma (visible) + high hidden "Cunning" (emotional manipulation trait based on Ego, Focus, Adaptability fusion) = sway locker room opinions and subtly sabotage others |
| **Booking Position and Historical Memory** | Long-standing legends or emotional anchors are harder to bury politically unless they self-destruct |
| **Road Agent/Booker Allies** | Workers aligned with emotionally powerful agents or co-bookers have hidden booking protection |

**Political power isn't visible on a chart. It's felt in how booking flows, how locker room murmurs, how media subtly frames stories.**

## How Political Wars Play Out âœ… LOCKED

| Stage | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **1. Subtle Undermining** | Political players push for their rivals to be booked into unfair losses, bad match pairings, cold feuds |
| **2. Alliance Building** | Faction loyalty intensifies. Workers orbit powerful figures emotionally and career-wise. |
| **3. Booking Drift** | Over time, rival workers see slower pushes, worse matches, harsher betrayal arcs. Not always obvious unless you're looking for patterns. |
| **4. Public Story Leaks** | Media "rumors" emerge about backstage favoritism, resentment, booking corruption |
| **5. Locker Room Drift or Revolt** | If political wars become open enough, locker room fractures solidify, exoduses happen, or company soul collapses |

Some political wars burn quietly for years. Others explode in open schisms.

| Risk | Consequence |
|------|-------------|
| **Overreach** | Political players who get too greedy spark visible revolt. Fanbase and locker room can turn, destroying careers. |
| **Underdog Rebellions** | Workers quietly buried can build slow rebellion arcs. Smart bookers or fans rescue them if timing and emotional climate match. |
