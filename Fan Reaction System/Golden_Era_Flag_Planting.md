## 1. REQUIREMENTS TO BEGIN A GOLDEN ERA

| Requirement | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Fan Trust at Peak** | Fans deeply, emotionally believe in the promotion. Booking mistakes forgiven. |
| **Booking Emotional Resonance** | Titles, feuds, heroes, betrayals all carry real emotional weight. Fans live/die by outcomes. |
| **Cultural Timing Alignment** | Promotion either rides or creates a cultural wave (rebellion era, technical mastery era, purist revival era) |
| **Worker Arc Alignment** | Top stars' stories organically mirror or fuel real-world emotional needs |
| **No Recent Major Betrayals** | No mass fanbase trust breaks in immediate memory. Momentum flows cleanly. |
| **External Competition Fragmented** | Not required, but Golden Eras burn brighter when rival promotions are weak, chaotic, or culturally out-of-touch |

### Requirement Thresholds (Approximate)

| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Fan Trust** | 85+ (out of 100) |
| **Booking Consistency** | 2-3 years minimum |
| **Cultural Alignment** | Positive match with current World Cultural State |
| **Betrayal Cooldown** | No major betrayals in past 18 months |

## 2. CONDITIONS THAT PLANT THE GOLDEN FLAG

| Condition | Example |
|-----------|---------|
| **Era-Defining Match or Moment** | Austin wins at WM14. Okada beats Tanahashi clean. Misawa beats Jumbo. |
| **Cultural Symbol Ascension** | Austin becomes working-class rebellion idol. Tanahashi becomes savior of wrestling tradition. Hangman becomes everyman emotional catharsis post-Brawl Out. |
| **Fan Trust "Spillover"** | Even casual fans start buying in emotionally, beyond hardcore loyalists |
| **Booking Consistency Over Time** | 2-3 years minimum of emotional consistency, escalating arcs, and clean title integrity |

### Flag Planting Moment Types

| Type | Description | Prestige Boost |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| **Coronation Match** | New ace defeats legendary champion cleanly | +30 to +50 |
| **Redemption Arc Climax** | Long-suffering hero finally wins the big one | +25 to +40 |
| **Invasion Victory** | Home promotion defeats invading threat | +20 to +35 |
| **Cultural Lightning Rod** | Worker becomes symbol of broader movement | +35 to +50 |

---

## 3. WHAT HAPPENS WHEN A GOLDEN ERA BEGINS

| Behavior | Simulation Effect |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Fan Trust Locks at Maximum** | Booking mistakes tolerated. Stars get over easier. New titles born into prestige more easily. |
| **Talent Pipeline Strengthens** | Young workers debut into trust-drenched environment. Fans invest quicker, stronger. |
| **Cross-Promotion Magnetism** | Other promotions seek alliances. Dream matches feel meaningful, not cash grabs. |
| **Cultural Mythmaking Begins** | Fans, media, future historians begin mythologizing current era even before it ends. |
| **Worker Career Catapults** | Careers made during Golden Eras rise faster, achieve greater lifetime prestige. |
| **Future Expectations Rise** | Downside: future eras judged mercilessly by Golden Era memory if trust fades. |

### Golden Era Bonuses

| Bonus | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| **Trust Decay Resistance** | -50% to trust decay from minor issues |
| **Star Creation Speed** | +50% to popularity growth for rising stars |
| **Title Prestige Growth** | +25% to belt prestige from defenses |
| **Recruitment Appeal** | +30% to worker signing likelihood |
| **Media Coverage** | +40% to mainstream attention |

---

## 4. HOW GOLDEN ERAS END

| Ending Cause | Effect |
|--------------|--------|
| **Betrayals/Scandals** | Montreal Screwjob fractures trust. Brawl Out fractures AEW's dream boom. |
| **Booking Laziness/Drift** | Golden Era fans notice stagnation faster than cynical fans, because they once felt everything. |
| **External Cultural Shift** | World events no longer match emotional tone of promotion. Fans drift toward promotions that fit better. |
| **Worker Exodus or Retirement** | Core emotional figure leaves or burns out (e.g., Misawa leaving AJPW). |

### Ending Type Consequences

| Ending Type | Fan Response | Recovery Time |
|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Natural Fade** | Nostalgic mourning, loyalty preserved | 2-3 years to new era |
| **Betrayal Shatter** | Bitter cynicism, trust destroyed | 5-10+ years, may never fully recover |
| **Cultural Drift** | Confused disengagement | 3-5 years with cultural realignment |
| **Star Exodus** | Emotional vacuum, searching for new heroes | 2-4 years if new stars emerge |

## 5. HISTORICAL GOLDEN ERAS

| Promotion | Golden Era | Defining Elements |
|-----------|------------|-------------------|
| **WWF Attitude Era (1997-2001)** | Emotional rebellion, anti-corporate catharsis, Austin/Rock ascendance |
| **AJPW King's Road (1990-1997)** | Emotional escalation, protected titles, Misawa/Kawada/Kobashi generational arcs |
| **NJPW Modern Workrate (2012-2018)** | Okada, Tanahashi, Naito, Omega defining world-class technical and emotional storytelling |
| **ECW Early Era (1993-1997)** | Cult loyalty, creative anarchy aligned with anti-corporate sentiment |
| **AEW Early Wave (2019-2021)** | Genuine alternative emotional surge pre-fracture (still pending full historical judgment) |
| **NWA Territory Peak (1975-1985)** | Regional loyalty, traveling champion mystique, Flair as centerpiece |

|--------|-------------|
