## 1. TRUST PHASES OVER TIME

Promotions move through distinct trust phases based on accumulated booking decisions:

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Faith Era** | Fans believe. High emotional investment. Forgive minor errors. |
| **Skeptical Era** | Fans watch warily. Smaller mistakes magnified. Storylines must prove themselves again. |
| **Cynical Era** | Fans expect betrayal. Even good booking is doubted. Constant grumbling. |
| **Checked-Out Era** | Fans no longer engage deeply. View company as circus/brand, not emotionally meaningful culture. Hard to recover. |

### Phase Transition Logic

| Direction | Speed |
|-----------|-------|
| **Downward (Faith ‚Üí Checked-Out)** | Can happen rapidly from single catastrophic events |
| **Upward (Checked-Out ‚Üí Faith)** | Extremely slow, requires years of consistent redemption arcs |
| **Skipping Phases** | Major betrayals can drop trust multiple phases instantly |

## 2. CORE TRUST MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Simulation Behavior |
|----------|---------------------|
| **Trust Meter (Hidden)** | Each promotion has dynamic Fan Trust value (0-100) that shifts slowly or violently based on key events |
| **Trust Decay/Growth Curve** | Much easier to lose trust than rebuild it; recovery is slow even across multiple successful years |
| **Fan Trust Spillover** | Fans may migrate emotionally to other promotions if home trust collapses (e.g., WCW fans ‚Üí WWF during Monday Night Wars) |
| **Generational Memory** | Deep betrayals linger for generations; even if promotion later rebuilds, old wounds may be referenced by future fans and media forever |

### Trust Effects on Booking

| Trust Level | Effect |
|-------------|--------|
| **High Trust** | More patience during bad bookings, botches, injuries |
| **Low Trust** | Rebellion, crowd mutinies, fan mockery even during decent shows |
| **Worker Push Acceptance** | In low-trust environments, even beloved wrestlers struggle to get over |
| **"Dark Era" Memory** | Companies that had Dark Eras never fully escape that historical shadow unless incredible redemption arcs happen |

---

## 3. HOW TRUST IS BUILT OR LOST

### Trust Builders (Slow)

| Event | Trust Effect |
|-------|--------------|
| **Consistent Storytelling with Payoff** | Trust rises slowly |
| **Honourable Title Arcs** | Champions win/lose meaningfully; belts respected = deep fan loyalty grows |
| **Genuine Worker Loyalty and Respect** | Fans love seeing loyalty rewarded, not punished |
| **Logical Losses That Build Credibility** | Tommy Rich model: trust and heat index grow even without wins |
| **Careful Feud Escalation** | Betrayal ‚Üí climax ‚Üí closure = emotional resonance locks into memory |
| **Hot Streak Protection** | Promotions that string emotional arcs together hit "Golden Era" trust status |

### Trust Destroyers (Fast)

| Event | Trust Effect |
|-------|--------------|
| **Shooty Bullshit Matches** | Humiliations, farces = trust takes massive hits |
| **Overexposure of Titles or Stars** | Saturation burns fans out and lowers trust |
| **Public Betrayals** | Screwjobs, scandals = massive instant trust crashes (even if booking tries to cover it) |
| **Transparent Political Booking** | Fans sense when matches aren't organic; pushes built purely on politics (e.g., HHH 2002 era) cause trust erosion |

---

## 4. PATTERNED LAZY BOOKING (BOOKING MEMORY SABOTAGE)

Fans detect "pattern fatigue" when booking structures repeat without evolution. This is separate from single catastrophic events: it's the slow rot of repetition.

| Lazy Habit | Effect |
|------------|--------|
| **Same finish structure every week** | Emotional warmth collapses |
| **Overexposure of same few workers** | Stars burn out faster |
| **Mindless 50/50 parity** | Fans stop believing in feuds or pushes |
| **No escalation of feuds** | Stagnation causes fan detachment |
| **Random shocking swerves with no payoff** | Audience exhaustion, cynicism |

### Key Insight

Trust isn't only event-driven (scandals, screwjobs). It is also **booking pattern-driven** based on how promotions subconsciously train their audience to believe or disbelieve in what they see week after week.

Trust restoration requires fresh emotional arcs, logical escalation, believable victories and defeats, not just "hot matches" or "shock angles."

---

## 5. BOOKING PHILOSOPHY DRIFT

A promotion's entire philosophy (match lengths, finish types, story complexity) can drift based on booking patterns. Fans perceive this over time, noticing when a promotion becomes more "shock-dependent," "lazy," or "narratively rich."

This subtle long-term drift affects baseline fan trust even without major scandals.

| Drift Type | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| **Shock-Hunter Drift** (Russo era WCW) | Trust erosion despite good workers |
| **Prestige Builder Drift** (NJPW 2015-2019) | Slow but lasting trust and global respect |
| **Blood and Chaos Drift** (ECW) | Short-term spikes but fragile long-term trust without care |
| **Corporate Sanitization Drift** | Fans feel booking is "safe" and soulless |
| **Nostalgia Dependence Drift** | Over-reliance on returns, legends, callbacks |

---

## 6. NARRATIVE FRESHNESS REQUIREMENTS

For trust to grow (or even maintain), feuds, pushes, and arcs must feel like they breathe, not static copy-paste:

| Good Practice | Reward |
|---------------|--------|
| **Logical losses that build credibility** | Fan trust and heat index grows even without wins |
| **Careful escalation of feuds** | Emotional resonance locks into memory |
| **Hot streak protection** | Promotions hit "Golden Era" trust status |
| **Arc emotional payoff** | Payoffs must feel earned; slow builds explode best when nurtured carefully |
| **Crisis response transparency** | Post-scandal behavior must be transparent, not manipulative |

---

## 7. HISTORICAL EXAMPLES

| Promotion | Trust Arc |
|-----------|-----------|
| **WWF (1996-1998)** | Rebuilt trust post-Screwjob through genuine rebellion (Austin rise, McMahon villainy), not by pretending everything was fine |
| **WCW (1998-2000)** | Lost trust after Fingerpoke of Doom, shooty bullshit, belt farces. Fans never emotionally returned. |
| **AEW (2019-2021)** | Early trust boom with fresh storylines and sincerity; recent cracks forming post-Brawl Out and booking stagnation moments |
| **TNA (2005-2010)** | Early X-Division built hope. Jarrett's endless self-push destroyed long-term faith. Fans grew cynical despite match quality. |
