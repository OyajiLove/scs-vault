## Design Philosophy

Training transforms raw potential into realized ability. The system distinguishes between:

**Natural Talent:** Initial stat ceilings set at worker generation
**Developed Skill:** Earned improvement through work and experience
**Mentored Growth:** Accelerated learning from veteran guidance

A worker with low natural talent (ceiling 14) can reach that ceiling faster with good training than a high-ceiling worker (ceiling 18) who coasts. Training rewards engagement.

**With Tags System:**

- Mentor tags may transfer to proteges
- "Green" tag removed after development milestones

**With Aging System:**
- Young workers (18-24) have highest growth potential
- Peak years (28-35) see slowed growth
- Veteran years: mental stats may still improve, physical decline begins

**With Injury System:**
- Injuries interrupt training progress
- Long-term injuries may permanently reduce potential
- Return from injury requires retraining period
