## Overview

Experienced workers can mentor younger talent, accelerating their development and potentially transferring style tags. Mentor relationships create bonds that affect loyalty, morale, and backstage dynamics. The quality of the mentor directly impacts the protege's growth rate.

---

## Mentor Qualifications

Not every veteran can be an effective mentor:

| Requirement | Minimum | Ideal | Notes |
|-------------|---------|-------|-------|
| **Years Active** | 8+ | 15+ | Experience matters |
| **Ring Psychology** | 14+ | 17+ | Must understand the craft |
| **Charisma** | 10+ | 14+ | Communication ability |
| **Locker Room Reputation** | Neutral+ | Respected | Workers must want to learn from them |
| **Current Role** | Any | Active veteran/agent | Still engaged with wrestling |

**Disqualifying Traits:**
- "Locker Room Cancer" tag: Cannot mentor effectively
- High Paranoia (16+): Reluctant to share knowledge
- Low Morality + High Ego: May sabotage protege development

### Tag Transfer

Mentors can teach proteges their own style tags:

| Tag Type | Transfer Chance | Time Required |
|----------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Common Style Tags** | 70% | 6 months |
| **Rare Style Tags** | 40% | 12 months |
| **Unique/Personal Tags** | 20% | 24+ months |
| **Legendary Tags** | 10% | Career-long relationship |

**Requirements:**
- Protege must have minimum base stats to support the tag
- Active mentor relationship (matches together, training time)
- Mentor willing to share (affected by personality)
