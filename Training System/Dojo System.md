## Overview

The Dojo System represents the rigorous Japanese approach to training wrestlers. Unlike Western systems where workers often enter with prior experience, Japanese dojos take young athletes and forge them from scratch through years of demanding physical and mental conditioning. This produces technically sound, disciplined workers with strong fundamentals, but at high human cost.

## Dojo Characteristics

### Training Intensity

| Intensity Level | Hours/Day | Rest Days | Description |
|-----------------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| **Traditional** | 6-8 | 0-1/week | Classic puroresu brutality |
| **Moderate** | 4-6 | 1-2/week | Modern adaptations |
| **Corporate** (SWA) | 3-4 (men) | 2/week | GWA-influenced lighter approach |
| **Brutal** (QHW/Miss Mitsuko) | 8-10 | 0/week | Extreme, high washout rate |

### Training Phase Duration

| Phase | Duration | Focus | Survival Rate |
|-------|----------|-------|---------------|
| **Initiation** | 3-6 months | Basic fitness, attitude testing | 50-70% |
| **Fundamentals** | 6-12 months | Bumps, rolls, basic moves | 70-85% |
| **Development** | 1-2 years | Style development, ring time | 85-95% |
| **Young Lion** | 1-3 years | Dark matches, enhancement | 95%+ |
| **Graduation** | Variable | Excursion or main roster | N/A |

## Regional Dojo Variations

### IPWA Dojo (Ringstate: CCA-Aligned Japan)

- Traditional King's Road approach
- Elbow wars and knee-drops as rites of passage
- Extremely hierarchical: Young Lions serve seniors
- 3-4 year minimum before main roster

### SWA Dojo (Ringstate: GWA-Aligned Japan)

**Men's Division:**
- Lighter regimen than traditional (GWA influence)
- 6-8 hour days with proper rest
- Sports medicine, modern recovery
- Still rigorous by Western standards
- Emphasis on looks, charisma alongside ability

**Women's Division (Miss Mitsuko):**
- Brutal traditional methods despite pop presentation
- 5 AM starts, no days off for minor injuries
- Public humiliation for mistakes
- "Suffering builds legitimacy"
- Massive contradiction: trained brutally, marketed as idols

### QHW Dojo (Ringstate: Women's King's Road)

- Mandatory retirement at 28 doctrine
- Short peak window intensifies training urgency
- Career is sprint, not marathon
- Extreme conditioning, technical precision
- High washout rate, elite survivors

## Young Lion System

### Young Lion Role

Workers who complete dojo training but haven't graduated to main roster:

| Responsibility | Purpose |
|----------------|---------|
| **Ring Setup/Breakdown** | Humility, work ethic |
| **Chores for Seniors** | Hierarchy respect |
| **Dark Matches** | Ring experience |
| **Enhancement Matches** | Losing to build stars |
| **Road Travel with Veterans** | Learning by proximity |

### Young Lion Restrictions

| Restriction | Reason |
|-------------|--------|
| **Limited Move Set** | Must prove fundamentals first |
| **No Finishers** | Signature moves earned later |
| **Plain Black Gear** | Identity comes with graduation |
| **No Promos** | Voice earned through performance |
| **Must Lose Clean** | Building credibility for future |

### Graduation Criteria

| Requirement | Threshold |
|-------------|-----------|
| **Time as Young Lion** | 2-4 years minimum |
| **Match Count** | 100+ matches |
| **Senior Approval** | Veteran consensus |
| **Excursion Completed** | If promotion uses system |
| **Debut Match** | Ceremonial first main roster win |

## Lifestyle Tracker / Aging Modifier (#45)

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Drinkers/Smokers** | Deeper lines, sunken eyes, rougher aging curves |
| **"Hard liver" personalities** | Faster decline, reduced longevity |
| **Natural freaks** | Slower aging (genetic advantage) |
| **Wrestling Style** | Brawlers/deathmatch workers show more visible wear |

### Genetics/Natural Resistance

Some workers have genetic advantages or disadvantages:

| Type | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Scar-prone** | Visible scarring accumulates (Funk bled buckets) |
| **Scar-resistant** | Barely marks despite damage (Baba) |
| **Quick Healer** | Faster recovery times |
| **Fragile Constitution** | More susceptible to long-term damage |

### Visual Portrait Evolution

These factors affect how worker portraits change over time:
- Hard living = more weathered appearance
- Clean living = maintains youthful look longer
- Style damage = visible scars, wear patterns
- Lifestyle tags dynamically affect aging curves
