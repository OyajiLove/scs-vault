## Core Concept

In SCS, drafts are world-shaping emotional events, not just roster management tools.

They simulate moments of:
- Territory collapse
- Brand split survival
- Booking regime changes
- Desperation acts by struggling promotions
- Cross-promotional pacts gone wrong
- Fanbase realignment

**Drafts create permanent emotional scars and relationship fractures. They're moments of rebirth, theft, betrayal, and political warfare.**

---

## Draft Types âœ… LOCKED

| Type | Trigger | Emotional Context |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| **Territory Collapse Draft** | A territory dies or goes bankrupt | Surviving promotions descend like vultures. Workers emotionally re-anchor or drift depending on where they land. |
| **Brand Split Draft** | One promotion splits into multiple brands | Internal political fracture (kayfabe or real). Fan loyalty splits, stables are shattered, myth memory fractures. |
| **Desperation Draft** | A struggling promotion is granted first pick rights | Last-ditch survival attempt. Sparks resentment in stronger promotions and divides locker rooms. |
| **Fantasy Draft (Player/Scenario Mode)** | Player triggers hard reset or "What if?" mode | Great for starting fictional worlds. Forge founding myths based on early alignment decisions. |
| **Cross-Promotion Draft Pact** | Two companies agree to share talent pools | Mutual draft to create balance. Eventually breaks down when one side starts hoarding or sabotaging. |

**Each draft isn't just about who you pick. It's about what emotional war you just triggered.**

## Draft Mechanics âœ… LOCKED

| Layer | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Pick Order** | Based on scenario: worst-record first, coin toss, lottery draw, ego-driven bidding war |
| **Worker Willingness Flags** | Some workers resist draft picks if Loyalty, Traditionalism, or Morality conflicts exist |
| **Draft Memory** | How a worker was drafted affects their loyalty for years: Picked last = resentful. Passed over = bitter. Stolen = guilt-ridden or arrogant. |
| **Stable/Faction Drift** | If only part of a stable gets drafted, emotional loyalty webs fracture. Worker arcs shift. |
| **Fanbase Drift** | If a beloved homegrown talent is drafted away, emotional trust between promotion and fanbase can erode |
| **Promotion Identity Pressure** | If a promotion drafts a worker who doesn't fit their soul identity, they may be booed, buried, or emotionally exiled |

## Expanded Mechanics (Vol 5 lines 1082-1085) âœ… LOCKED

### Vulture Drafts (Territory Collapse) - Expanded

| Layer | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **Draft Pool** | All remaining contracted workers, staff, champions, and titles enter available pool |
| **Pick Order Logic** | Determined by regional proximity, power ranking, emotional loyalty flags (some feds refuse "tainted" names) |
| **Time Pressure** | Offers may be time-limited. Workers may disappear if not claimed fast. |
| **Worker Agency** | High Loyalty or Traditionalism = may refuse to report to a rival fed |
| **Title Myth Fallout** | If a belt is absorbed, it may collapse emotionally unless preserved properly |
| **Media Tone** | "Poachers." "Rebuilders." "Grave-dancers." Each fed's reputation shifts based on how they handle remains. |

**Real examples:** AJPW after NOAH exodus, WCW collapse into WWF, NWA territories folding in the '80s

### Desperation Drafts (Fed on Brink) - Expanded

| Layer | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **Drafted Workers' Reactions** | May resent going to sinking ship. May become saviour figures or bolt immediately. |
| **Fanbase Split** | Desperation drafting seen as cynical or heroic. Depends on soul fit. |
| **Post-Draft Fallout** | May trigger locker room friction or scandals if ego/workload spikes too fast |
| **Optional Restraints** | "Must only draft unsigned workers" / "Can only take 1 star from each rival" / "Can trade picks but not workers" |

**Real examples:** TNA begging for stars in 2005, early MLW rebuilding through scraps

### Brand Drafts (Split/Rebrand) - Expanded

| Layer | Behavior |
|-------|----------|
| **Protected List** | Each brand can lock in X number of talents before draft begins |
| **Unprotected Pool** | All other workers eligible. Players/AI must make hard emotional calls. |
| **Draft Style Options** | Classic round-by-round, "grab and hold," or randomized chaos |
| **Fan Reaction** | Depends on perceived integrity of split. Political = backlash. Earned (after stable wars) = support. |
| **Booking Pressure** | Belts may split, unify, or collapse depending on post-draft treatment |
| **Worker Memory** | "I wasn't protected." "They left me behind." Loyalty and morale shift instantly. |

Option to soft-split (division-based) or hard-split (full roster separation).

## Post-Collapse Talent Redistribution (Vol 5 lines 1086-1093) âœ… LOCKED

**Renamed from "Vulture Draft"** to avoid gamey feeling.

Also called: "Open Pool Frenzy," "Talent Grab Phase," "Soul Salvage Phase"

### Key Design Principles

- **OFF by default** (toggle on in setup or post-collapse world event options)
- NOT a formal draft, presented as chaotic industry scramble
- Media frame it as "blood-in-the-water moment," not organized event
- Handles collapse the way wrestling actually does: messily, politically, emotionally

### How It Works

| Mechanic | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Trigger** | Promotion collapses (financial ruin, scandal, internal revolt, soul death) |
| **Presentation** | "Following the collapse of PNW Pro, a free-for-all scramble has begun across the region." |
| **No Pick Order** | AI feds evaluate who to approach. Workers respond emotionally. Media react based on who "scavenged" vs "rescued." |
| **Worker Agency** | Some refuse to sign quickly (stunned, bitter, holding out). Others jump instantly. |
| **Title Handling** | Titles float, collapse, or vanish depending on treatment |

### Access Rules (Customizable)

- Only regional feds may claim talent
- Only promotions with scouting tags can see value in lesser-known talent
- First-come, first-served with negotiation skill and relationships mattering

### Optional Custom Triggers (Advanced Settings)

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Protect Belt Myths** | Major titles myth-locked unless transferred via worker with emotional legitimacy |
| **Enable Talent Grab Pressures** | Media criticize aggressive grabbing. Impacts fan loyalty or commentary tone. |
| **Chaos Clause** | Two+ feds after same worker triggers booking war or locker room poaching feud |
| **Full Draft Override** | Player can manually initiate structured redistribution draft, with backlash risk |
