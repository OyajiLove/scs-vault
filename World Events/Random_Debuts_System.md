## Core Concept

In historical start modes, workers don't just appear on a fixed schedule. The Random Debuts System controls when future workers enter the world, with options ranging from strict historical accuracy to full chaos.

**Default philosophy:** Era matters. Region defaults matter. Player control is sacred.

## Philosophy

> "Random debuts bring your historical sims to life. They reflect world drift, fan hunger, promotion opportunity gaps, and worker hidden 'Drive' stats. They allow stars to rise organically, not just from 'known' picks. And it works perfectly in both standard and fantasy draft historical start modes."
