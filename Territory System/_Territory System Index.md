## Design Philosophy

The territory system existed historically (NWA territories, 1950s-1980s) and is central to Ringstate's 1985 setting. Territories aren't just geographic markers; they're cultural ecosystems with distinct identities, crowds, and politics.

**Key Principles:**

1. **Regional Identity Matters:** A worker's reception differs dramatically between Memphis and New York
2. **Local Kings:** Territorial Kingpins wield real power in their home region
3. **Outsider Dynamics:** Visiting talent face different crowd expectations
4. **Cultural Memory:** Territories remember betrayals, invasions, and heroes differently
5. **Economic Variation:** Territory size and TV deals determine earning potential
