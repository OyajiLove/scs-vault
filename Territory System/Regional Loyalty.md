## Overview

Regional Loyalty tracks how strongly a worker is connected to a specific territory. This affects their popularity, political power, booking preferences, and willingness to move. Some workers are "territory lifers" who will never leave home; others are "road warriors" who have no territorial attachment.

---

## Loyalty Attributes

### Home Territory

Every worker has a designated home territory based on:
- Birth/origin location
- Training location (if different)
- Career start location (if different)

**Multiple Homes:** Some workers develop secondary home territories through long stays.

### Loyalty Rating

| Rating | Level | Description |
|--------|-------|-------------|
| **1-3** | Rootless | No territorial attachment, will go anywhere |
| **4-6** | Mobile | Prefers home but will travel for opportunity |
| **7-9** | Attached | Strong home preference, reluctant to leave |
| **10-12** | Loyal | Very strong attachment, needs major reason to leave |
| **13-15** | Devoted | Deeply tied, would sacrifice career for territory |
| **16-20** | Territorial Kingpin | Identity fused with territory, will never leave |

### Loyalty Factors

| Factor | Effect on Loyalty |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Years in territory** | +0.5 per year |
| **Title reigns in territory** | +1 per major reign |
| **Family in territory** | +2 to +5 |
| **Trained in territory** | +2 |
| **Born in territory** | +3 |
| **Business interests** | +1 to +3 |
| **Bad experience elsewhere** | +1 to +2 |
| **Betrayal by territory** | -3 to -5 |
| **Better opportunity elsewhere** | -1 to -3 |

## Loyalty Effects

### On Popularity

| Scenario | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Working in home territory** | +10 to +20 pop bonus |
| **Away from home, friendly territory** | +0 (neutral) |
| **Away from home, hostile territory** | -10 to -20 pop penalty |
| **Returning home after absence** | +5 to +15 "homecoming" bonus |

### On Booking

| Scenario | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **High loyalty worker** | May refuse outside bookings |
| **Territorial Kingpin** | Can block outsider pushes |
| **Low loyalty worker** | Takes any booking |
| **Territory ace** | Promotion depends on them staying |

### On Politics

| Scenario | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **In home territory** | +15 to +25 influence bonus |
| **Away from home** | -10 influence penalty |
| **Multiple homes** | Influence split between territories |

### On Contract Negotiations

| Scenario | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **High loyalty + home promotion** | Accepts lower pay |
| **High loyalty + outside offer** | Needs major premium to leave |
| **Low loyalty** | Pure mercenary calculus |

## Loyalty Archetypes

### Territory Lifer

**Profile:**
- Loyalty: 16-20
- Has never worked outside home territory
- Identity fused with regional promotion
- Will sacrifice national stardom for regional king status

**Examples:** Jerry Lawler (Memphis), Bill Watts (Mid-South)

**Behavior:**
- Rejects outside offers even for more money
- Demands top spot in home territory
- May sabotage outsiders who threaten position
- Becomes face of territory in collective memory

### Road Warrior

**Profile:**
- Loyalty: 1-4
- Has worked many territories
- No strong attachment to any location
- Values opportunity over roots

**Examples:** Harley Race, Terry Funk (early career)

**Behavior:**
- Goes where money/opportunity is best
- Adapts to different territory styles
- Never becomes truly "local" anywhere
- May be distrusted by territory lifers

### Adopted Son

**Profile:**
- Loyalty: 10-15 (to adopted territory)
- Started elsewhere but became local icon
- Has "earned" territorial status through years

**Examples:** Stan Hansen (Japan), Dusty Rhodes (Florida)

**Behavior:**
- Fiercely protective of adopted home
- May have conflicted feelings about origin
- Crowds treat as native despite origins
- Can bridge territories politically

## Territorial Kingpin Mechanics

Workers with Territorial Kingpin archetype (HIDE-001) have special rules:

| Mechanic | Effect |
|----------|--------|
| **Booking Pull** | Can influence booking in home territory |
| **Outsider Resistance** | Can block pushes for non-local workers |
| **Gate Power** | Territory needs them for attendance |
| **Political Protection** | Difficult to fire or demote |
| **Morale Impact** | Territory-wide morale tied to their status |

**Kingpin Triggers:**
- Worker must have 16+ Loyalty to territory
- Must have been main eventer for 3+ years
- Must have significant local popularity (75+)
- Must have high Ego (14+) and high Traditionalism (15+)
