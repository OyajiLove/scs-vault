## Overview

Territories are geographic regions with distinct wrestling identities, crowd behaviors, and promotional ecosystems. Each territory has historical significance, cultural characteristics, and mechanical effects on gameplay.

---

### Traditional CCA Territories

## Territory Attributes

### Crowd Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Range |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| **Memory Duration** | How long crowds remember events | 1-10 years |
| **Style Preference** | Favored wrestling style | Technical/Brawl/High Fly/etc. |
| **Heat Threshold** | How easily heel heat generates | Low/Medium/High |
| **Outsider Reception** | How foreign talent is received | Hostile/Neutral/Welcoming |
| **Kayfabe Respect** | How seriously kayfabe is taken | Traditional/Smart/Mixed |

### Economic Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Range |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| **Market Size** | Population/potential audience | Small/Medium/Large |
| **TV Penetration** | Cable/broadcast coverage | Low/Medium/High |
| **Gate Potential** | Average attendance capacity | Low/Medium/High |
| **Merch Culture** | Merchandise purchasing tendency | Low/Medium/High |

### Political Attributes

| Attribute | Description | Range |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| **Faction Alignment** | Dominant Ringstate faction | CCA/GWA/PWU/CFPW/etc. |
| **Independence** | Resistance to outside control | Low/Medium/High |
| **Booker Power** | Local booker influence | Weak/Moderate/Strong |

## Example Territory Profiles

### The Deep South

```
Territory: Deep South
Regions: Georgia, Alabama, Mississippi, Tennessee (excluding Memphis)
Major Cities: Atlanta, Birmingham, Nashville

Crowd Attributes:
  Memory Duration: 8 years (never forget)
  Style Preference: Brawling, Blood Feuds, Southern Rasslin'
  Heat Threshold: Low (easy to get heat)
  Outsider Reception: Hostile (earn your place)
  Kayfabe Respect: Traditional (don't break it)

Economic Attributes:
  Market Size: Medium
  TV Penetration: Low (but growing)
  Gate Potential: Medium-High (passionate audiences)
  Merch Culture: Low (come for wrestling, not shopping)

Political Attributes:
  Faction Alignment: CCA (traditional)
  Independence: High (resists GWA invasion)
  Booker Power: Strong (local promoters have control)

Special Rules:
  - Outsiders start with -20 pop penalty
  - Blood matches get +15% crowd response
  - Heel/face alignment must be clear (no tweeners)
  - Betrayals create 5+ year grudges
```

### Northeast Corridor

```
Territory: Northeast Corridor
Regions: NYC, Boston, Philadelphia, Connecticut
Major Cities: New York City (MSG), Boston Garden

Crowd Attributes:
  Memory Duration: 3 years (faster turnover)
  Style Preference: Spectacle, Characters, Big Names
  Heat Threshold: High (harder to get pure heat)
  Outsider Reception: Neutral (prove yourself)
  Kayfabe Respect: Smart (smarks emerging)

Economic Attributes:
  Market Size: Large
  TV Penetration: High (media center)
  Gate Potential: Very High (MSG draws)
  Merch Culture: High (consumer culture)

Political Attributes:
  Faction Alignment: GWA (corporate HQ)
  Independence: Low (GWA controlled)
  Booker Power: Weak (centralized decisions)

Special Rules:
  - National stars get +10 pop bonus
  - Pure wrestling > brawling in crowd response
  - MSG shows get x1.5 prestige multiplier
  - Media coverage affects pop faster
```

## Territory Boundaries (Conceptual)

**Boundary Disputes:**
- Some workers claimed by multiple territories
- Boundary promotions may work both sides
- GWA expansion creates new contested zones

**Boundary Effects:**
- Workers from boundary areas: Dual loyalty possible
- Shows in boundary areas: Mixed crowd expectations
- TV coverage may cross boundaries
