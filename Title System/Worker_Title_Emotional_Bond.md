## Core Concept

In SCS, titles aren't just belts. They are emotional artifacts tied to:
- Workers
- Promotions
- Crowd loyalty
- Match memory

Certain workers become emotionally fused with specific titles. Losing, betraying, or abusing that emotional bond can either build myths or rot emotional loyalty permanently.

**Workers and titles co-mythologize each other.**
