## Core Concept

**Titles Aren't Just Belts:**
In SCS, titles serve as emotional artifacts, carrying a legacy of triumph, honor, and struggle. Over time, if a champion overuses a certain title narrative (relying too heavily on one signature storyline or repeatedly "selling" the same injury), it risks losing its emotional potency.

**Myth Decay:**
When a title's emotional resonance is overexploited without evolution, the myth surrounding the title begins to decay. This manifests as:
- Lower fan excitement
- Diluted world memory
- Loss of the title's protective aura

**Reluctant Partner Stage:**
As decay reaches critical point, the burden of the title becomes too much for the worker to bear alone. They enter a "reluctant partner" phase where they must team up with another worker to mount even a semblance of a credible challenge against emerging threats.

**Death Riders:**
Aggressive, next-generation workers or factions that exploit the weakened myth. They represent the brutal evolution of the sport, arriving with overwhelming force and modern booking tactics that the decayed champion can no longer face solo.

## Process and Mechanics âœ… LOCKED

### 1. Myth Decay Accumulation

| Factor | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Emotional Overexposure** | The more a title is defended using the same tired narrative (perpetually selling same injury without evolution), the more myth decays |
| **Fan Memory Erosion** | As title becomes stale, emotional weight lessens, weakening champion's aura and protection |
| **Booking Consequences** | Continuous long reigns with repetitive storylines result in decreased trust from fanbase |

### 2. Onset of Reluctant Partner Stage

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Champion's title myth reaches decay threshold (tracked via cumulative emotional decay metrics)
- Worker performance in carrying title's legacy begins to suffer (visible in emotional and physical fatigue)

**Forced Collaboration:**
- System forces scenario where decaying title-holder cannot face top-tier challengers alone
- Approached by management (or emerging instinct) to form tag team/pairing with another worker
- Partnership is reluctant: born of necessity, not loyalty or camaraderie
- Goal: fend off the looming threat of "death riders"

### 3. Confrontation with Death Riders

**Death Rider Emergence:**
- Rival workers or factions seize upon champion's vulnerability
- Known for raw aggression, modern booking sophistication, ruthless efficiency

**Match Dynamics:**
The decaying champion and reluctant partner must combine strengths:
- Champion contributes residual legacy and worn emotional scars
- Reluctant partner brings fresh energy and potential
