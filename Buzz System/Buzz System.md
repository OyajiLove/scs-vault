## Overview

> "Buzz builds fast. It decays faster. It burns hot, and sometimes burns out."

A short-term energy metric reflecting crowd interest, momentum, speculation, danger, unpredictability, stakes, and freshness.

---

## What Buzz Is

A short-term energy metric for:
- Workers
- Titles
- Feuds
- Segments
- Match Types

**Responsive to:**
- Match quality and outcome unpredictability
- Promos, angles, run-ins, betrayal, controversy
- Booking memory (title defenses, feud turns, weird pairings)
- Era, region, audience culture

---

## What Buzz Is NOT

- Not a replacement for Pop (long-term star power)
- Not a stat â€” it's a sim memory flag and heat meter
- Not guaranteed to convert into money or overness
- Not immune to burnout, backlash, or sabotage

## Buzz Decay Curves

Each Buzz-holding entity has a decay profile, measured per show (or per week). Decay can be slowed, frozen, or reversed by booking activity, but never indefinitely halted.

| Entity | Base Decay Rate | Burnout Risk | Notes |
|--------|-----------------|--------------|-------|

## Burnout Flags

When Buzz decay hits a certain threshold after a peak, the system triggers a burnout state, reducing future Buzz gains unless something resets perception.

| Type | Burnout Trigger | Effect |
|------|-----------------|--------|
| Worker | Drops 6+ in 3 shows | Next angle gains Buzz at half-rate |
| Feud | Spikes then stalls with no payoff | Crowds stop reacting until repackaged |
| Promotion | High Buzz â†’ repeat booking for 3â€“5 shows | All Buzz gains slow until variety introduced |
| Match Type | Used more than twice in same feud/run | Flat reactions even if match quality is high |
