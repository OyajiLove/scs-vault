## Overview

Different media outlets serve different purposes in wrestling coverage. Each has distinct reach, credibility, audience, and relationship to kayfabe. Understanding outlet types is essential for managing publicity and scandal.

---

## Outlet Categories

### Mainstream Media

**Characteristics:**
- Large audience reach
- Low wrestling-specific knowledge
- Treats wrestling as entertainment/sport
- Covers major events, scandals, celebrity crossover
- Kayfabe-adjacent (doesn't expose business)

| Outlet Type | Reach | Credibility | Coverage Focus |
|-------------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **National TV News** | Very High | High | Major events, scandals |
| **Local TV News** | Regional | Medium | Local shows, community |
| **National Newspapers** | High | High | Business, scandals |
| **Local Newspapers** | Regional | Medium | Event coverage |
| **Entertainment Magazines** | Medium-High | Low-Medium | Celebrity angle |
| **Sports Media** | Medium | Medium | Athletic angle |

### Wrestling-Specific Media

**Characteristics:**
- Wrestling-focused audience
- Deep industry knowledge
- Kayfabe varies (magazines protect, dirtsheets expose)
- Covers in-ring, backstage, business

| Outlet Type | Reach | Credibility | Kayfabe Status |
|-------------|-------|-------------|----------------|
| **Wrestling Magazines** | Medium | Medium | Protects kayfabe |
| **Dirtsheets (print)** | Low-Medium | Variable | Exposes business |
| **Newsletter (Meltzer-style)** | Low | High (among smarks) | Full shoot |
| **TV Guide/Listings** | High | Low | Kayfabe (treats as show) |

### Modern Era Additions (Post-1995)

| Outlet Type | Era | Reach | Characteristics |
|-------------|-----|-------|-----------------|
| **Wrestling Websites** | 1997+ | Growing | Real-time, insider access |
| **Podcast/Audio** | 2005+ | Niche | Deep dives, shoot interviews |
| **Social Media** | 2008+ | Viral | Worker-direct, unfiltered |
| **YouTube** | 2007+ | Massive | Highlight clips, documentaries |
| **Streaming/Reddit** | 2012+ | Niche | Community-driven analysis |

## Outlet Profiles (1985 Era)

### The Wrestling Observer Newsletter

**Type:** Dirtsheet/Newsletter
**Founded:** 1983
**Editor:** Dave Meltzer (real-world reference)
**Reach:** Low (hundreds of subscribers)
**Credibility:** Very High (among industry insiders)

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Kayfabe Status** | Full shoot (exposes everything) |
| **Audience** | Smart fans, industry insiders |
| **Coverage Focus** | Business, backstage, ratings, match quality |
| **Scandal Handling** | Reports everything, no filter |
| **Industry Impact** | High (shapes smart fan perception) |

### Pro Wrestling Illustrated

**Type:** Magazine
**Reach:** High (newsstand distribution)
**Credibility:** Medium

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Kayfabe Status** | Full kayfabe (treats as real sport) |
| **Audience** | Casual fans, collectors |
| **Coverage Focus** | Rankings, interviews (kayfabe), photos |
| **Scandal Handling** | Ignores or kayfabe-explains |
| **Industry Impact** | Medium (shapes casual perception) |

### Local Sports Section

**Type:** Newspaper
**Reach:** Regional
**Credibility:** Medium-High

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Kayfabe Status** | Kayfabe-adjacent (treats as show) |
| **Audience** | General public |
| **Coverage Focus** | Event listings, occasional features |
| **Scandal Handling** | Only if mainstream newsworthy |
| **Industry Impact** | Low (limited coverage) |

## Outlet Attributes

### Reach

| Level | Definition | Audience Size |
|-------|------------|---------------|
| **Very High** | National/International | 1M+ |
| **High** | National | 100K-1M |
| **Medium** | Regional or niche national | 10K-100K |
| **Low** | Local or highly niche | 1K-10K |
| **Very Low** | Underground | <1K |

### Credibility

| Level | Definition | Belief Rate |
|-------|------------|-------------|
| **Very High** | Trusted industry source | 90%+ |
| **High** | Respected outlet | 70-89% |
| **Medium** | Taken with grain of salt | 50-69% |
| **Low** | Entertainment value only | 30-49% |
| **Very Low** | Known for inaccuracy | <30% |

### Kayfabe Relationship

| Status | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Full Kayfabe** | Treats wrestling as real |
| **Kayfabe-Adjacent** | Acknowledges show, doesn't expose |
| **Mixed** | Sometimes breaks, sometimes protects |
| **Shoot** | Exposes business openly |
