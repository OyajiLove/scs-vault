## 7. IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

When implementing these systems:

1. **Track hidden meters** for all emotional states (not shown to player directly)
2. **Use probability rolls** with personality modifiers for outcomes
3. **Log everything to world memory** (nothing is forgotten)
4. **Allow cascade events** to ripple through connected systems
5. **Provide subtle player feedback** (atmosphere cues, not explicit warnings)
6. **Make recovery possible but slow** (years, not months)
