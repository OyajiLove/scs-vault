## Core Concept

**"Start with a destination match (the blow-off), then book backwards from there."**

This reflects real wrestling logic: Gedo, Dusty, and other legendary bookers knew where they wanted to end up, then built the road to get there.

A Trail is not a railroad. It's a path with flexibility, milestones, and emotional checkpoints.
