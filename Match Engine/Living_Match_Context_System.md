## The Core Problem LMC Solves

**Without LMC:**
- "Book Omega vs Okada" = mechanical match assignment
- No context awareness
- Same match feels same regardless of circumstances

**With LMC:**
- "Book exhausted, emotionally fraying Okada vs hungry, destiny-seeking Omega during collapse of Okada's invincible aura"
- Every match embedded in living context
- Same workers, completely different emotional drama

---

## Historical Model: Okada vs Omega (G1 2017)

| Arc Element | Emotional Layer |
|-------------|-----------------|
| **Okada's state** | Not just "wrestling worse." Emotionally and physically drained by unstoppable reign. Crowd knew it. |
| **Omega's state** | Not just "won." Earned it against a wounded god. Victory meaningful, not procedural. |
| **Crowd response** | Emotionally rode the fatigue. Every Okada comeback felt desperate and brave. Every Omega attack felt heavier. |
| **Memory anchor** | Became legendary precisely BECAUSE of context, not just match quality |

**Core Insight:** Fatigue accumulation is a storytelling engine, not just a debuff.

## Four Living LMC Layers âœ… CANON

All four layers are alive simultaneously and influence each other dynamically:

| Layer | Meaning | Impact |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **Macro Fan Climate** | Global emotional drift (hope, collapse, rebellion eras) | Baseline fan patience and mood over years |
| **Micro Show Climate** | Live crowd emotional drift per night: fatigue, hope, betrayal, restlessness | How matches are received moment to moment |
| **Worker Fatigue/Wear** | Accumulated physical exhaustion, scar tissue, chronic pain, psychological stress | How workers physically move and emotionally act in matches |
| **Booking Memory and Trust** | World memory of betrayal arcs, brutal wars, scandal damage | How promotions are perceived long-term by fans, workers, media |

**Integration:** No isolation. No mechanical dead pools. True human aging, trauma, myth-building. All four breathe together naturally.
