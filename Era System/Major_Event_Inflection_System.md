## INFLECTION EVENTS (TRIGGERING CATALYSTS)

Moments where something goes too real or too viral to ignore:

| Event | Potential Result |
|-------|------------------|
| **Public fallout with top star** (Bret, Punk, etc.) | May destroy morale... or turn you into a heel boss character |
| **Worked shoot goes off rails** | Fans may revolt or crown the speaker a cult hero |
| **Network complaint about a segment** | May force tone shift... or give you a reputation for edginess |
| **Major injury used in angle** | Risk backlash... or become a legendary revenge arc |
| **Union busting leak** | May burn trust... or draw in anti-union sponsors who love the ruthlessness |
| **Face turns on fans in real life interview** | Heel alignment "sticks" and changes show tone |
| **Booker caught leaking or sabotaging** | May create factional war, and a compelling storyline if embraced |

---

## CANONICAL EXAMPLE: MONTREAL SCREWJOB

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Scandal** | Public fallout with top star + exposed finish |
| **Fan Reaction** | Betrayal + outrage |
| **WWF Response** | Vince leans in, turns heat into heel persona |
| **Outcome** | New DNA era born. Attitude was no longer "coming." It was here. |

**In SCS, this is what separates reactive players from narrative architects.**

## INFLECTION RESPONSE OPTIONS

| Option | Result |
|--------|--------|
| **Acknowledge & Incorporate** | DNA shifts toward reality fusion, fans get hooked |
| **Deny & Sanitize** | May avoid short-term loss, but fans may rebel |
| **Exploit Boldly** | Sponsorship risk skyrockets, but buzz soars |
| **Overcorrect** | Tone flattens. Risk of crowd disconnect. |
| **Let Others Tell the Story** (podcasts, dirt sheets) | Identity begins evolving without your consent |
