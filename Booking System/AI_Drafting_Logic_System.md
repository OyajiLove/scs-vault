## Core Concept

AI-controlled promotions don't just pick "the best available worker." They draft based on:
- Promotion soul & founding myth
- Booking style preference
- Locker room chemistry
- Ego management
- Popularity vs control tension
- Long-term emotional arcs

So no, Vince isn't drafting Misawa early. Because his brain is screaming: "Doesn't fit the style. Doesn't fit the locker room. Can't control him."
