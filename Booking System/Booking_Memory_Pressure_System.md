## Core Concept

Promotions, locker rooms, and audiences develop emotional booking instincts over time. Not clean mechanical "push best worker by stats" logic. Past emotional events warp future decisions.

Memory lives inside booking tendencies organically.

---

## Sources of Booking Memory Pressure âœ… LOCKED

| Memory Type | Booking Influence |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Past Betrayals** | Workers who stabbed companies/fanbases risk booking hesitation, shorter title reigns, early betrayal arcs planned against them |
| **Emotional Scars** | Workers who survived brutal arcs have emotional fan shields. Killing them off poorly creates backlash. |
| **Fatigue/Decay Visibility** | Workers visibly declining physically (without late-career adaptation) risk quiet burial bookings or forced tragic collapse arcs |
| **Fanbase Emotional Loyalty** | Bookers pressured to protect fan-favorite survivors or risk crowd betrayal (AEW Hangman 2021 style pressures) |
| **Promotion Identity Drift** | If the company soul shifts (rebellion era, tragedy era), workers emotionally out of sync with new soul risk faster burial |
| **Worker Cultural Drift** | Workers who become memes or ironic figures (too many sympathy rehashes, old legends clinging on) face passive or active burial |

---

## Practical Booking Effects Over Time âœ… LOCKED

| Booking Layer | Behavior |
|---------------|----------|
| **Worker Title Shot Trust** | Workers with strong fan emotional loyalty are trusted with longer, later title reigns, even post-physical peak |
| **Booking Hesitation** | Workers with fan fatigue, betrayal scars, or physical visible decay see slower title pushes, more midcard insulation |
| **Forced Redemptions** | Promotions can try to forcibly "rehabilitate" a broken myth. Risk of backlash if emotional timing misreads. |
| **Emotional Anchor Protection** | Workers beloved emotionally but physically decayed may get protected final runs, gentle send-offs, career curtain calls |

The emotional weight of past booking shapes future instincts naturally.

## How AI Bookers Handle Memory Pressure âœ… LOCKED

| Pressure Type | Behavior |
|---------------|----------|
| **High Pressure to Protect Fan Emotional Icons** | AI avoids screwing beloved legends unless deliberate tragic booking arcs planned |
| **High Betrayal Memory Weight** | AI subtly limits betrayers. Books betrayals against them faster, keeps them emotionally vulnerable. |
| **Promotion Drift Awareness** | AI smart enough to recognize if old stalwarts clash with new rebellion/nostalgia/tragedy climates. Books transitions emotionally if possible. |

Not mechanical "popularity" stat booking. True emotional instinct booking.

## Player Experience of Booking Memory Pressure

As a player, you will feel the emotional weight:
- Booking certain workers "wrong" will spark subtle or overt fan revolt
- Protecting emotionally beloved workers properly will make booking easier, smoother, more emotionally resonant
- Trying to push emotionally broken myths will risk hurting your show's loyalty trajectory, not just tanking one event

**Booking memory becomes an emotional survival game, not just a "ratings chase."**
