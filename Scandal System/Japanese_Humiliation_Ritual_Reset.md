## 1. WHEN RITUAL CAREER RESETS HAPPEN

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Severe Embarrassment to Promotion** | Losing to outsiders, disgraceful match quality, real-world scandal tainting the brand |
| **Personal Humiliation** | Massive losing streaks, being exposed as frauds, disrespecting tradition openly then being broken |
| **Voluntary Penance** | Worker offers to humble themselves to preserve honour and signal true contrition: not ordered by office |
| **Promotion Cultural Setting** | Far more likely in Japanese promotions, old-school traditionalist companies (AJPW, NJPW 70s-2000s). Rare elsewhere. |

### Trigger Thresholds

| Trigger Type | Threshold |
|--------------|-----------|
| **Embarrassment to Promotion** | Major loss to outsider OR 3+ consecutive embarrassing incidents |
| **Personal Humiliation** | 10+ consecutive losses OR major public exposure |
| **Voluntary Penance** | Humility score 70+ AND Loyalty score 60+ |
| **Cultural Setting** | Japanese promotion OR traditionalist flag active |

---

## 2. FORMS OF PENANCE (4 RITUALS)

| Ritual | Description | Severity | Recovery Time |
|--------|-------------|----------|---------------|
| **Head Shaving** | Symbolic surrender of pride and status. Public statement: "I have failed." | High | 6-12 months |
| **Reversion to Young Lion Status** | Stripped of personal gimmick/branding. Returns to plain black trunks, basic matches, dojo chores. Starts rebuilding trust from zero. | Maximum | 1-3 years |
| **Public Ceremony of Humility** | Formal, sincere public apology or in-ring admission of failure | Medium | 3-6 months |
| **Exile for Reflection** | Voluntarily leaves for a time: dojo exile, overseas learning excursion, or freelance wandering | Medium-High | 6-18 months |

### Ritual Selection Factors

| Factor | Preferred Ritual |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Scandal Severity: Minor** | Public Ceremony |
| **Scandal Severity: Moderate** | Head Shaving OR Exile |
| **Scandal Severity: Major** | Young Lion Reversion |
| **Worker Age: Young** | Young Lion Reversion acceptable |
| **Worker Age: Veteran** | Head Shaving or Exile (Young Lion reversion humiliating) |
| **Worker Status: Main Eventer** | Any ritual is major news |

## 3. HISTORICAL MODELS

| Worker | Ritual Event | Outcome |
|--------|--------------|---------|
| **Shinya Hashimoto** | Head shaved after humiliating losses to Naoya Ogawa. Voluntary self-humbling. | Partial redemption; career complicated by politics |
| **Kazuchika Okada (Early NJPW)** | Post-TNA "Rainmaker Disaster" returns humbled to rebuild as a serious star. Emotional reset, not formal head shaving. | Full redemption into Immortal Icon |
| **Various Dojo Graduations** | Workers "graduate" from Young Lion status by proving humility and growth, not just time served | Standard career progression |

### Hashimoto Case Study

**Context:** Hashimoto, NJPW ace, lost repeatedly to MMA fighter Naoya Ogawa in worked shoots designed to legitimize Inokiism. The losses humiliated him publicly.

**Response:** Hashimoto voluntarily shaved his head in public ceremony, acknowledging his failures and beginning a penance arc.

**Outcome:** Partial fan trust recovery, but career never fully recovered due to political situation and Inokiism damage to NJPW.

## 4. SIMULATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Worker Humility Score Check** | Only workers with strong humility and loyalty values will offer true penance voluntarily |
| **Fan Trust Boost (Long-Term)** | True rituals deepen fan respect dramatically if done sincerely: emotional forgiveness rises faster |
| **Short-Term Brand Damage** | Momentary embarrassment to promotion reputation (unless culturally accepted, like Japan) |
| **Career Rebuild Chance** | Workers who submit to ritual resets may organically rebuild stronger, more deeply respected careers |
| **Booking and Push Freeze** | Promotions may require reset workers to reprove themselves through lower card grinds before main event reentry |

### Trust Recovery by Ritual Type

| Ritual | Trust Recovery Rate | Maximum Trust Ceiling |
|--------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| **Head Shaving** | +15/month if sincere | 85% of pre-scandal |
| **Young Lion Reversion** | +20/month if sincere | 95% of pre-scandal |
| **Public Ceremony** | +10/month if sincere | 75% of pre-scandal |
| **Exile for Reflection** | +12/month if sincere | 80% of pre-scandal |

## 5. SPECIAL RULES

| Rule | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Botched or Insincere Rituals** | Workers faking humility (based on hidden personality scores) actually worsen fan betrayal. Trust drops additional -20. |
| **Rare Western Acceptance** | In American/Western promotions, attempts at public humility rituals often backfire or are mocked unless booked with extreme care and sincerity. 70% backfire rate. |
| **Promotion-Ordered vs Voluntary** | Voluntary penance is 2x more effective than promotion-ordered. Fans sense the difference. |
| **Repeat Offenders** | Second ritual attempt is 50% less effective. Third attempt nearly worthless. |

### Western Backfire Conditions

| Condition | Backfire Likelihood |
|-----------|---------------------|
| **Promotion has cynical fanbase** | +30% backfire |
| **Worker has low authenticity reputation** | +40% backfire |
| **Media frames it as PR stunt** | +50% backfire |
| **Ritual feels performative** | +60% backfire |
| **Worker has high authenticity + sincere delivery** | -40% backfire |

---

## 6. PHILOSOPHY

> "True humility costs something. Emotional forgiveness must be earned, not demanded. Penance reopens the path, but the walk must still be made."

The ritual is not the redemption. The ritual is permission to attempt redemption. The worker must still prove themselves through sustained behavior change, lower card grinding, and authentic growth.
