## Overview

This document catalogs the historical wrestling archives available for SCS development. These materials provide authentic data for:
- Worker skills evolution
- Chemistry/archetype mapping
- Scandal real-world baselines
- Fanbase evolution tracking
- Promotion philosophies
- Regional wrestling psychology differences

**Core Truth:** This is not just a database. It's the foundations of a living wrestling civilization.

## Primary Archives

### Wrestling Observer Newsletter (1982-2021)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Format** | Weekly PDFs (~50 issues/year) |
| **Coverage** | 1982-2021 (2000+ issues) |
| **Size** | ~2GB compressed |
| **Strength** | Deep match analysis, star ratings, backstage news, business coverage, scandal documentation |
| **Bias** | Generally skeptical of management; defends business-as-business |

**Special Resource:** 236-page Wrestling Observer Index (1982-2010) for targeted searches without drowning in raw data.

**Observer Yearbooks:** Concentrated annual records of scandals, match ratings, deaths, backstage events, business shifts.

### PW Torch Newsletter (Through 2022)

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Format** | Weekly newsletters |
| **Coverage** | Through 2022 |
| **Strength** | Alternative perspective to Observer; more ethical angle coverage; stronger worker-first tone |
| **Bias** | More ethical outrage; more player-friendly, fan-rights-focused |

**Value:** Counterbalance to Meltzer's Observer; catches stories Dave misses; strong American promotion and political dynamics coverage.

### Torch vs Observer Comparison

| Factor | Observer | Torch |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **Scandal Sensitivity** | High when affecting booking/business; sometimes light on moral implications | Very high on wrestler health, safety, dignity |
| **Match Analysis** | Star ratings + story importance | More psychology and historical place discussion |
| **Business News** | Strong business tracking | Good editorializing, less sharp than Observer |
| **Worker Politics** | Generally neutral | Slightly more progressive/worker-first |

**Best Practice:** Blend Torch + Observer for most human and full reconstruction of wrestling world.

## Modern Sources (2022-Present)

For post-2022 coverage when Observer/Torch access is limited:

| Source | Strengths | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Voices of Wrestling** | Strong analysis of Japanese wrestling, indies, WWE/AEW; critical eye; less corporate bias | Year-end reviews, match reviews, scandals analysis |
| **Fightful (Fightful Select)** | Good insider scoops (contracts, backstage heat, injuries, scandals) | Some paywall content |
| **Post Wrestling** | Calm, professional coverage of scandals, backstage drama, business trends | Canadian-focused but international coverage |
| **VoW Annual Match Guides** | Top matches, promotion performances, worker ascensions/falls | Good fan sentiment gauge |
| **Cagematch.net** | Fan consensus, career trajectory ratings, match quality ratings | Not journalism but strong sentiment indicator |
| **The Ringer Pro Wrestling** | Culturally serious coverage | Mainstream bias possible |
| **Reddit r/SquaredCircle** | Crowdsourced scandal threads, event fallout | Bias and trolling exist |
| **DVDVR Board Archives** | Indie/puro experts; incredible depth | Smaller communities |

**Wrestlenomics (Brandon Thurston):** Strong for business news, ratings analysis, financial trends.

## Usage Strategy

### Don't Mass Upload

The archives are too large for bulk upload. Use surgical targeting:

| Need | Strategy |
|------|----------|
| **Career Arcs** | Pull major years for specific workers (e.g., Bret Hart 1996-1997) |
| **Scandal Deep Dives** | Pull focused clusters (e.g., Bill Watts racism scandal year) |
| **Promotion Changes** | Pull periods of transition (e.g., WCW sale 2001) |
| **Era Research** | Pull decade clumps as needed |

### Batch Upload Guidelines

| Size | Guideline |
|------|-----------|
| **10-15 Observer PDFs** | Perfect for 1 upload batch (~50-80MB) |
| **Big Story Year** | Maybe 1 focused zipped folder per major event |
| **Decade Recaps** | 1-2 targeted zipped pulls if needed |

### Index-First Approach

Use the 236-page Observer Index (1982-2010) as the "table of contents" to identify specific issues needed, then pull only those.
