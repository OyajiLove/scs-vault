## Overview

Arena Prestige represents how significant a venue is to wrestling history and current perception. High prestige venues make shows feel more important, affect worker performance, and command higher expectations. A title change at MSG means more than the same title change at a bingo hall.

---

## Prestige Scale

| Level | Range | Descriptor | Examples |
|-------|-------|------------|----------|
| **Legendary** | 90-100 | "The Cathedral" | MSG, Tokyo Dome, Korakuen Hall |
| **Historic** | 70-89 | "Major Arena" | Budokan, Greensboro Coliseum |
| **Significant** | 50-69 | "Regional Hub" | Cow Palace, ECW Arena |
| **Standard** | 30-49 | "Typical Venue" | Local arenas, civic centers |
| **Minor** | 10-29 | "House Show Venue" | Small halls, gyms |
| **Minimal** | 0-9 | "Whatever Works" | Bingo halls, parking lots |

---

## Prestige Factors

### Positive Factors

| Factor | Prestige Boost |
|--------|----------------|
| **Historic events held** | +2 to +5 per major event |
| **Years of wrestling** | +1 per decade |
| **Capacity (large)** | +5 to +15 |
| **Iconic architecture** | +5 to +10 |
| **Media visibility** | +5 to +15 |
| **Championship venue** | +5 to +10 |
| **Sellout frequency** | +3 to +8 |

### Negative Factors

| Factor | Prestige Loss |
|--------|---------------|
| **Failed shows** | -2 to -5 per failure |
| **Renovation/Closure** | -5 to -15 (temporary) |
| **Bad memories** | -3 to -10 |
| **Competition with new venue** | -5 to -10 |
| **Neighborhood decline** | -5 to -10 |

---

## Prestige Effects

### On Match Significance

| Venue Prestige | Match Significance Modifier |
|----------------|----------------------------|
| **Legendary** | 1.5x to 2.0x |
| **Historic** | 1.2x to 1.4x |
| **Significant** | 1.1x to 1.2x |
| **Standard** | 1.0x |
| **Minor** | 0.9x |
| **Minimal** | 0.7x to 0.8x |

### On Worker Performance

| Effect | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Big Match Overperformer** | +5% to +15% at legendary venues |
| **Pressure Cooker** | Benefits from high-prestige stress |
| **Choker** | May underperform at major venues |
| **Hometown Bonus** | +10% at home venue |

### On Crowd Expectations

| Venue Prestige | Crowd Expectation |
|----------------|-------------------|
| **Legendary** | Expect legendary matches |
| **Historic** | Expect quality, meaningful booking |
| **Standard** | Normal expectations |
| **Minor** | Lower expectations, easier to impress |
