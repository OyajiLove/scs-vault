## Overview

The Legacy System tracks a worker's career imprint as perceived by fans, bookers, media, and peers. It blends fact, memory, and emotion to determine Hall of Fame induction, retirement reception, posthumous legend status, and documentary treatment.

> "He wasn't the best worker, but you felt something when he walked out."
> "She changed the scene. You can't rewrite history without her."
