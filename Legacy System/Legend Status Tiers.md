## Overview

Beyond the numeric Legacy Score, workers can achieve special status designations that affect how they're treated by fans, bookers, media, and the game itself.

---

## Status Tiers

| Status | Conditions | Examples |
|--------|------------|----------|
| **Legacy** | Built via Buzz, Memory, Arcs. Numeric score (1-100). | Most workers |
| **Legend** | Designation unlocked via HOF induction + ongoing cultural impact | HOF inductees with sustained relevance |
| **Myth** | Reserved for once-a-generation icons. Requires: Immortal legacy (90+) + multiple disciplines, eras, or transcendent cultural impact | Hogan, Austin, Misawa, El Santo |

---

## Status Effects

| Status | Effect |
|--------|--------|
| **Legacy (score only)** | Standard treatment, score affects booking and HOF |
| **Legend** | Commentary uses special language, younger workers reference them, return pops boosted |
| **Myth** | Near-permanent cultural fixture, death triggers massive memorial, can define entire eras in retrospective |

---

## Myth Criteria (Vol 6 Implied)

To achieve Myth status, a worker typically needs:
- Legacy Score 90+ (Immortal tier)
- Multiple title reigns across eras OR multiple promotions
- Defining moments that transcend wrestling (cultural crossover)
- Death or retirement that became a cultural event
- Ongoing influence on style, booking, or industry structure
