## BOOKING BEHAVIOR

- Promo time is essential: these workers need mic segments to build threat
- Matches should be paced slower with psychological storytelling
- Pre-match promos increase effectiveness
- Best feuds are long-burn psychological warfare
- Often work better as final boss of multi-match arc
- Can elevate babyfaces by making them overcome fear

## DESIGN NOTES

### Dark Weaver vs. Other Archetypes

**vs. Heel Ace:**
Heel Aces dominate through skill, charisma, and championship-level credibility. Dark Weavers dominate through psychological warfare. A Heel Ace like Flair makes you feel inferior. A Dark Weaver like Jake makes you feel afraid.

**vs. Monster:**
Monsters use size and physical menace. Dark Weavers use psychological menace. Yokozuna scares you with his mass. Jake scares you with his words.

**vs. Showman (Broken Entertainer):**
Some crossover exists with Broken Entertainer (Broken Matt Hardy, Firefly Funhouse Bray). The distinction: Broken Entertainers are postmodern/comedic first with darkness as flavor. Dark Weavers are darkness first with possible comedic elements as weapon.

### Archetype Name Workshop (Vol 2)

Considered alternatives before locking Dark Weaver:
- Serpents (too Jake-specific)
- Corrupters (too aggressive)
- Whisperers (softer, haunting)
- Tempters (too religious)
- Shadowspeakers (too mystical)

**Dark Weaver** chosen for: poetic, mythic, implies spinning dark fates through words and deeds.
