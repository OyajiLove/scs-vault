## MATCH DNA: FIGHTING SPIRIT

| Element | Game Implementation |
|---------|---------------------|
| **Offensive Momentum System** | Fast shift momentum engine: dominant offense rewarded |
| **Submission Threat Level** | Submissions have live threat throughout match, not just wear-down |
| **Sudden Finish System** | Finishes can happen at any time (no guaranteed multi-kickout drama) |
| **Aggression Modifier** | Bonus to strike flurries, comeback flurries |
| **Spirit Bomb Tag** | Comebacks possible but dangerous: taking two death moves in a row could still kill you if spirit not triggered |

**In gameplay terms:** Matches feel urgent, punishing, more like a fight than a choreographed escalation.

## FIGHTING SPIRIT VS. KING'S ROAD

| Aspect | Fighting Spirit (NJPW) | King's Road (AJPW/NOAH) |
|--------|------------------------|-------------------------|
| Pace | Higher tempo, aggressive, urgent | Slower build, gradual escalation |
| Offense vs Defense | Offense-focused (attack, dominate, overwhelm) | Defense-focused (resist, survive, counter) |
| Match Flow | Less predictable, finishes can happen at any point | Predictable structure: slow base ‚Üí injury tease ‚Üí big escalation |
| Move Philosophy | Submissions and strikes credible at all times | Big escalation, head drops, exhaustion finishes |
| Emotion | Spirit shown in sudden survival, desperate comebacks | Spirit shown through enduring increasing punishment step-by-step |
| Injury Risk | Moderate, tied to submission damage or KO | Extremely high in final escalation stages |
| Psychology | Martial arts/military realism emphasis | Classical storytelling, mythmaking, mortality in-ring |

## DESIGN NOTES

### Black Charisma Subtype (Vol 2 Chunk 02)

Black Charisma (Chono-type heel aura) was originally considered for Heel Ace but relocated to Fighting Spirit as it better represents NJPW heel ace energy within the fighting spirit ecosystem. These workers combine the endurance and presence of Fighting Spirit with a dark, menacing aura: cool, calculated dominance without relying on monster size or cheap heat. They're still about survival and struggle, just from the heel perspective.

### Hybrid Potential

Fighting Spirit workers often have hybrid potential with:
- Junior Puroresu (early career before heavyweight transition)
- Brawler (violence-focused variants)
- King's Road (cross-promotion style clashes)
