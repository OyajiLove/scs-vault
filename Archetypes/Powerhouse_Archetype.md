## CORE SKILLS (MANDATORY)

| Skill | Priority | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| **Power** | Very High (Core) | Strength feats, intimidating presence |
| **Believability** | Very High (Core) | Without it, looks silly and cartoonish |
| **Brawling** | Medium-High | Natural extension: stiff strikes, clubbing blows |
| **Charisma** | Medium | Separates top guys (Goldberg, Batista) from filler |
| **Durability** | Low-Medium | Variable: many breakdowns, bad gas tanks |
| **Psychology** | Low | Varies heavily by worker |
| **Basics** | Low | Often clunky unless evolved later |

### Why This Skill Balance?

| Trait | Reason |
|-------|--------|
| Power | Obvious: strength feats, intimidating presence |
| Believability | Fans need to feel you could actually dominate |
| Brawling | Natural extension of power: stiff strikes |
| Charisma | Helps separate top guys from filler monsters |
| Durability | Highly variable: roid injuries, gas tanks |
| Psychology/Basics | Often rough unless properly carried/booked |

## SUBTYPES

| Subtype | Core Concept | Examples |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| **Juggernaut** | Unstoppable offensive machine. Constant, relentless power. | Goldberg (WCW era), early Brock Lesnar |
| **Freak Athlete** | Unreal athletic gifts combined with size and strength. Freakishly quick AND freakishly strong. Scary hybrid guys who can run 40s in crazy time and deadlift 500 pounds. | Brian Cage, early Bobby Lashley, Oba Femi |
| **Grizzled Strongman** | Traditional big bruisers who rely on "tough man" contests. Pride in toughness. Old-school brute strength. | Jim Duggan, early Ron Simmons, Stan Hansen (power side) |
| **Squash Monster** | Built for short, devastating matches. Used to shock and awe crowds, sometimes as gatekeepers. Domination specialists. | Ryback, early Braun Strowman, Umaga (early) |
| **Big Fight Specialist** | Powerhouses who specialize in peaking for big showdowns. Great at promos, weigh-ins, big-match feel. Major event, marquee powerhouses. | Batista, Brock Lesnar (later), Drew McIntyre (post-comeback) |

## ASSOCIATED TAGS

| Tag | Meaning |
|-----|---------|
| **Strength Freak** | Enhanced crowd reactions for feats of strength (slams, presses, throws) |
| **Short Match Boost** | Bonus effectiveness in matches under 10 minutes (Squash Monster types) |
| **Momentum Engine** | Gathers momentum quickly when winning: harder to stop once rolling |
| **Power Pop** | Finisher or huge slam generates immediate crowd spike, even if match isn't a classic |

## BOOKING BEHAVIOR

- Often transitional champions
- Dominant midcard champion material
- Monster slayer storylines
- Big fight attractions for marquee events
- Squash matches build mystique
- Protect in longer matches (carry them)
- Gas tank issues common

### Career Arc Patterns

**Peak:** Short reign of dominance, mythic aura  
**Decline:** Injuries, diminishing returns, exposed in long matches  
**Late Career:** Nostalgia attraction or behind-scenes role
