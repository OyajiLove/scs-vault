## Tweener (#233)

**Core Skills:** Believability (absolute must), Charisma (must draw attention without clear alignment)

**Flexible Skills:** Any in-ring style can be a tweener depending on story

**Subtypes:**
| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Reluctant Heel | Forced into villainy | Seth Rollins Authority turn |
| Reluctant Face | Doesn't want to be hero | Eddie Kingston |
| Chaotic Neutral | Pure unpredictability | Stone Cold '97 |
| Self-Interested Opportunist | Out for themselves | Edge, MJF |
| Antihero | Dark methods, crowd support | Moxley, Cactus Jack ECW |
| Silent Tweener | Alignment unclear through silence | Asuka, early Samoa Joe |

**Fan Reaction:** Highly volatile, earned through performance not loyalty

**Era Sensitivity:** More natural post-80s, Attitude Era onwards

**Booking Notes:** Strong booking = beloved. Mishandling = rejected as fake.

## Heel Ace (#234)

**Core Skills:** Charisma (command audience), Psychology (control match flow), Overness (must be top tier in region/nation)

**Optional Skills:** Promo varies (silent killer heels vs talkative)

**Subtypes:**
| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Arrogant Champion Heel | Smug, dominant | Flair 80s, Roman Tribal Chief |
| Silent Monster Heel | Actions speak | Lesnar 2014-15, Vader |
| Cerebral Assassin Heel | Strategic, manipulative | HHH Reign of Terror |
| Charismatic Dictator Heel | Magnetic tyrant | Hollywood Hogan, MJF |
| Ruthless Shooter Heel | Legitimate threat | Suzuki |

**Fan Reaction:** Hate + grudging respect = money

**Booking Sensitivity:** Must lose rarely but meaningfully

**NOTE:** Kawada is NOT Cerebral Assassin. He needs Defiant Soul archetype.

## Travelling Heel Champion Subtype (#236)

**Parent Archetype:** Heel Ace

**Core Role:** Elevate local babyfaces across promotions while protecting world title prestige

**Core Skills:** Charisma (make faces look good through struggle), Psychology (pace matches for drama), Technical (optional)

**Booking AI:**
- Prioritize close matches with local babyfaces
- Escape wins (roll-ups, foot on ropes, distracted refs)
- Rare clean losses only to legend-level faces

**Fan Impact:** Audience expects heartbreak not squash

**Example:** Ric Flair (definitive example)

---

## Travelling Heel Monster Subtype (#237)

**Parent Archetype:** Monster

**Core Skills:** Menace (overwhelming), Power (physically dominant), Durability (take punishment)

**Booking AI:**
- Beat midcarders clean
- Beat lower babyfaces brutally
- Build final showdown vs top local face
- After big loss, move on immediately

**Fan Impact:** Early excitement. If overstaying after loss, fans cool quickly.

**Examples:** Brody, Hansen, The Sheik, Abdullah, Ernie Ladd

## Storyteller Archetype (Reference #225)

**Note:** Mostly serves as HYBRID LAYER over other archetypes. Pure storytellers are rare.

**Core Skills:** Psychology, Selling

**Examples:**
- Peak 80s Steamboat = Pure Storyteller
- Young Akiyama = Pure Storyteller
- Kobashi = Monster + Storyteller
- Bret = Technician + Storyteller
- Paul Orndorff = Heel + Storyteller (masterful heel heat segment control)

**Design Note:** Rather than a standalone archetype, Storyteller functions as a tag or modifier applied to primary archetypes.
