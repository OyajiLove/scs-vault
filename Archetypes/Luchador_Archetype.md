## Lucha Booking Culture (#30 - Locked)

### Special Mechanics

| Feature | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Family Protection Tag** | Harder to book legacy workers jobbing |
| **No Yob Clause** | Some workers refuse to lose without major negotiation |
| **Regional Loyalty** | Won't travel easily outside home territory |
| **Mask Loss Sensitivity** | Must be negotiated carefully, career-defining |

### Booking Negotiation

Mask matches and title shots with top masked workers require special negotiation screens. Unmasking is NOT routine, it's a major career event.

## Trios Culture

Lucha libre emphasizes trios (3v3) more than singles:
- Tag IQ and teamwork highly valued
- Faction loyalty stronger than US/Japan
- Trios titles often prestigious
