## Booking Considerations

- Technicians shine in longer matches (15+ minutes)
- Work best against other technicians or brawlers (style contrast)
- Heel technicians can work 60-minute draws effectively
- Pure technicians may struggle with crowd heat unless paired with charismatic opponents
- Era sensitivity: Modern fans expect faster pace than 80s technician matches
