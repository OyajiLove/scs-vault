| Subtype | Description | Examples |

### Pretender Heel Subtype (#262)

**Definition:** Pure facade, no real danger behind bluster, HAVE to cheat because not real threat  
**Stats:** Low Believability (8-10), polished "play actor" corporate vibes  
**Example:** The Miz

## Wildcard (#260)

**Core Skills:** Charisma 14-20, Believability 15-20, Brawling 14-20, Basics 8-15, Psychology 10-16, Selling 12-18, Mic Fluency 10-16

**Subtypes:**
| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| Unhinged Brawler | Full chaos | Buzz Sawyer, Wild Bull Curry |
| Calculated Wildman | Appears chaotic but uses it as weapon | Funk, Brody |
| Violence Artist | Bleeds realness, violence feels too real | New Jack, prime Hansen |

**Notes:** Chaos engines who don't follow normal wrestling rules inside or outside kayfabe. Big match feel with no polished structure, mayhem/emotion/wildness over technical flow. Dangerous physicality, anti-establishment aura.

**Examples:** Bruiser Brody, Buzz Sawyer, Terry Funk, Stan Hansen (early), New Jack, Wild Bull Curry

**Parent:** Territory Lifer  
**Core Skills:** High Psychology, High Believability, Strong Mic Skills (gritty, not polished)  
**Booking:** Thrive on long builds, betrayal angles, personal feuds  
**Tags:** Master of Heat (heel), Master of Sympathy (face), Territorial Grind Expert  
**Example:** Eric Embry

### Ham and Egger Subtype (#253)

**Definition:** Crockett/territory workers who looked barely trained, slobs, got beat up  
**Key Stats:** Basics 1-6, Selling 4-10, Safety 2-8, Believability 1-7  
**Purpose:** Visual squash dominance for stars (Vader, Brody, Hansen, Nikita looked terrifying murdering these guys)  
**Tags:** Sloppy Worker, Tough As Nails, Human Punching Bag, No Threat, Occasional Comedy

## Enforcer/Technical Bruiser (#255)

**Core Skills:** Technical 14-19, Brawling 14-18, Psychology 14-20, Basics 15-20, Believability 14-20

**Full Skill Ranges:**
| Skill | Range |
|-------|-------|
| Athleticism | 6-13 |
| Flashiness | 1-6 |
| Mic Skills | 6-14 |
| Selling | 12-18 |
| Stamina | 12-18 |
| Durability | 14-20 |
| Menace | 10-18 |

**Tags:** Ring General, Grinder, Limb Killer, No Nonsense Persona

**Notes:** Methodical punishers, technically sound but technique geared towards inflicting punishment, grinding opponents down, injuring. NOT hybrid, distinct archetype. Long grinding heat segments, limb work, relentless pressure, realistic struggle.

**Examples:** Arn Anderson, Ole Anderson, Finlay (pure exemplar), Regal (80% Enforcer, 20% Technician), Buzz Sawyer, Dax Harwood
