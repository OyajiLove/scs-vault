## Subtypes

### Vol 1 Foundation

| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Arrogant Champion Heel** | Smug, cheating champion | Ric Flair (80s), Roman Reigns (Tribal Chief) |
| **Silent Monster Heel** | Dominant destroyer | Brock Lesnar (2014-15), early Vader AJPW |
| **Cerebral Assassin Heel** | Manipulative, political | Triple H (Reign of Terror) |
| **Ruthless Shooter Heel** | Legitimate threat | Suzuki (NOAH/NJPW) |

### Vol 2 Additions

| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Arrogant Monarch** | Traveling ace, believes they are born to rule. Technical excellence, psychological domination. The traveling "I'm better than you" heel ace. | Ric Flair (NWA era), Nick Bockwinkel, prime Jay White |
| **Dominant Egotist** | Physically capable champion who wins by any means necessary, often using underhanded tactics even when superior. Could win clean but too narcissistic to risk it. | Hollywood Hogan (nWo era), Roman Reigns (early Bloodline arc) |
| **Charismatic Outlaw** | Cool heel who bends/breaks the rules but oozes charisma and natural crowd pull. Slouchy, dangerous without ever seeming desperate. | Kevin Nash, Scott Hall, Eddie Guerrero (early heel runs) |
| **Scheming Mastermind** | Heel ace who relies heavily on mind games, manipulation, strategic cheating. Slick, manipulative, smart rather than overpowering. | MJF, Tully Blanchard, early Christian Cage |
| **Territorial Tyrant** | Local kingpin heel who owns his turf and runs the scene with iron charisma and dirty tricks. The hometown villain everyone loves to hate but who still packs the building. | Jerry Lawler (heel Memphis runs), Bill Dundee (early AWA/Memphis) |

## Travelling Heel Subtypes (#24)

### Travelling Heel Champion

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Identity | Elite worker elevating local babyfaces across promotions |
| Core Skills | Charisma, Psychology, Technical (optional) |
| Booking AI | Close matches, escape wins, rare clean losses |
| Fan Impact | Heartbreak expected, not squash |
| Territory Role | Champion travels, makes territory babyfaces |
| Examples | Ric Flair (NWA), Harley Race |

### Travelling Heel Monster

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Identity | Large brutes demolishing roster, building heat |
| Core Skills | Menace, Power, Durability |
| Booking AI | Beat midcarders clean, brutal babyface losses, move after big loss |
| Fan Impact | Early excitement, cools quickly if overstaying |
| Territory Role | Short runs, maximum destruction, leave after top babyface wins |
| Examples | Bruiser Brody, Stan Hansen, Abdullah the Butcher |
