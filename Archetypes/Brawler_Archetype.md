## Subtypes

| Subtype | Description | Examples |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| **Dirty Brawler** | Eye pokes, low blows, cheating | Roddy Piper, early Terry Funk |
| **Heroic Brawler** | Blue collar fighting spirit | Austin, Tommy Dreamer |
| **Technical Brawler** | Strikes + mat work | Finlay, Dick Murdoch |
| **Psychotic Brawler** | Unhinged violence | Cactus Jack, prime Terry Funk |
| **Hardcore Brawler** | Weapons, blood, chaos | Moxley, Onita, Abdullah |
| **Wildman Brawler** | Animalistic, unpredictable | Brody, Umaga, Stan Hansen |
| **Grimy Enforcer** | Midcard bruiser who handles dirty work for heel factions, stable protectors, or company enforcers. Role-players who enforce stables, storylines, heel dominance. (Vol 2 addition) | Meng, Dick Murdoch, early Ron Simmons |
