## 1. WHEN A PROMOTION ENTERS SURVIVOR STATE

| Trigger | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Golden Era Collapse Confirmed** | Emotional center of the fanbase shattered: trust at critical low |
| **Mass Worker Exodus or Loss** | Icons gone, emotional anchors fractured or buried |
| **Fanbase Exodus or Fracture** | Crowds shrink, emotional loyalty thins, nostalgia cults form |
| **Revenue Collapse Risk** | Merchandise, TV ratings, gate attendance pressure mounting: promotion survival depends on adaptation |
| **New Cultural Landscape** | External world no longer aligned with previous booking style or brand values |

### Survivor State Confirmation

| Condition | Required |
|-----------|----------|
| **Trust Level** | Below 30% for 12+ months |
| **Worker Roster** | Lost 50%+ of main event talent |
| **Fanbase** | Lost 40%+ of core audience |
| **Revenue** | Declined 35%+ from peak |
| **Cultural Alignment** | Below 40% |

**All 3+ conditions must be met for Survivor State confirmation.**

## 2. SURVIVOR STRATEGIES (5 PATHS)

| Strategy | Description | Short-Term | Long-Term |
|----------|-------------|------------|-----------|
| **Pander to Nostalgia** | Rely heavily on Golden Era branding (legends appearances, old logos, tribute events) | Boost | Rot risk |
| **Cultural Pivot** | Complete shift in style (workrate revolution, edgier product, realism boom) to realign with cultural mood | Risky | Potential rebirth |
| **Budget Collapse Adaptation** | Strip down production, tighten roster: shift to indie model survival mode | Stable | Limited ceiling |
| **Heel Turn Against Fans** | Embrace cynicism; book for heat and outrage instead of loyalty: creates anti-fan cult identity | Dangerous | Niche survival |
| **Slow Authentic Rebuilding** | Rebuild through real arcs, protected titles, genuine new hero cultivation | Very slow | True resurrection possible |

### Strategy Selection Factors

| Factor | Favored Strategy |
|--------|-----------------|
| **Financial desperation** | Budget Collapse Adaptation |
| **Strong legacy assets** | Pander to Nostalgia |
| **Cultural moment shifting** | Cultural Pivot |
| **Toxic fanbase remaining** | Heel Turn Against Fans |
| **Patient ownership** | Slow Authentic Rebuilding |

## 3. HISTORICAL MODELS

| Promotion | Survivor Path | Outcome |
|-----------|---------------|---------|
| **WWE Post-2007** | Slow Authentic Rebuilding | Rebuilt after Benoit tragedy, steroids, fanbase cynicism. Used Cena, PG rebrand, nostalgia mining. Partial success. |
| **AJPW Post-NOAH Split** | Budget Collapse â†’ Slow Rebuilding | Barely survived early 2000s as skeleton. Slow prestige rebuild through Mutoh, Suwama, Miyahara decades later. |
| **TNA/Impact Post-2013** | Budget Collapse + Rebrands | Budget collapse, multiple rebrands (Impact Wrestling), total reworking of brand identity over 10+ years. Surviving. |
| **ECW Post-PPV Boom** | Budget Collapse | Attempted indie survival mode but died before completing transition. Failed survivor. |
| **WCW 1999-2001** | Failed all strategies | Tried nostalgia, pivots, and desperation. Nothing worked. Collapsed completely. |

### AJPW Case Study

**Collapse:** NOAH exodus (2000) stripped AJPW of Misawa, Kobashi, Taue, Akiyama, and most of the roster. Baba dead. Motoko Baba's leadership contested.

**Survivor State:** Entered full Survivor State by 2001. Trust at floor. Roster skeleton.

**Strategy:** Budget Collapse Adaptation initially, then Slow Authentic Rebuilding over 15+ years.

**Key Moments:**
- Mutoh signing (2002): brought star power
- Suwama development: new ace cultivation
- Miyahara rise (2010s): genuine new hero

**Outcome:** True Resurrection by late 2010s, but never returned to King's Road peak. Memory fracture persists.

## 4. SIMULATION MECHANICS

| Mechanic | Behavior |
|----------|----------|
| **Survivor Status Tag** | Promotions flagged in history: "Survived Collapse 2005-2020" |
| **Brand Trust Decay** | Trust levels much slower to rebuild. Fans treat new rises skeptically until long arcs restore investment. |
| **Worker Loyalty Dynamics** | Young workers entering post-collapse don't inherit loyalty: must forge new bonds from scratch |
| **Fan Nostalgia Decay** | Overreliance on nostalgia booking slows decay but accelerates rot after diminishing returns hit |
| **Promotion Death Risk Remains** | Survivor promotions can still collapse outright later if strategies fail or compound rot |

### Trust Rebuild Rates by Strategy

| Strategy | Trust Gain per Year | Maximum Ceiling |
|----------|--------------------|-----------------| 
| **Pander to Nostalgia** | +5 (diminishes to +1) | 50% of peak |
| **Cultural Pivot** | +8 if successful, -15 if rejected | 80% of peak |
| **Budget Collapse** | +2 | 30% of peak |
| **Heel Turn** | +3 (niche only) | 25% of peak |
| **Slow Authentic Rebuilding** | +10 | 90% of peak |

### Nostalgia Diminishing Returns

| Years of Nostalgia Mining | Trust Gain Modifier |
|---------------------------|---------------------|
| **1-2 years** | 100% effectiveness |
| **3-4 years** | 75% effectiveness |
| **5-7 years** | 50% effectiveness |
| **8+ years** | 25% effectiveness + rot risk |

## 5. TRUE RESURRECTION VS UNDEAD SURVIVAL

| State | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **True Resurrection** | Rare. Promotions rebuild through real trust arcs. New stars, protected titles, genuine emotional investment returns. | AJPW (partial, Miyahara era), WWE (partial, 2016-2019) |
| **Undead Survival** | Promotions technically alive but emotionally hollow. Brand exists, fans attend, but no deep emotional loyalty. Empty spectacle. | Late WCW, certain modern feds |

### Resurrection Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| **New Ace Development** | Must create at least 1 new main event star organically |
| **Protected Title Prestige** | World title must regain credibility |
| **Sustained Trust Growth** | Trust must grow 3+ consecutive years |
| **Cultural Relevance** | Must reconnect with current cultural mood |
| **Authentic Storytelling** | Must deliver emotionally resonant arcs, not just nostalgia |

## 7. PHILOSOPHY

> "Survival is not a triumph by itself. Survival can be strength: or rot. Only emotional authenticity can resurrect. Otherwise, you simply exist as a ghost of your former self."

A Survivor Promotion is not just a promotion that didn't die. It's a promotion living with the scars of collapse, fighting to prove it deserves to exist. Some never escape Undead Survival. Some, through patience and authentic storytelling, achieve True Resurrection. The simulation must capture both possibilities.
