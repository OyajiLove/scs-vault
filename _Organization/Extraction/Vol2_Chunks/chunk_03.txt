## PHYSICAL SKILLS CLARIFICATION

### Legitimacy vs Believability (CRITICAL DISTINCTION):
- **Legitimacy** = How good in a REAL fight (shoot fighting ability)
  - Examples: Minoru Suzuki (20), Brock Lesnar (20), CM Punk (low)
  - Category: Pure Physical Attribute

- **Believability** = How CONVINCING their performance is (acting/presence)
  - Examples: Jake Roberts (20), The Miz (8-10), Hulk Hogan (high for style)
  - Category: Performance/Acting Attribute

### Final Core Skill Map:
**Physical:** Power, Athleticism, Durability, Stamina, Legitimacy
**Performance:** Aerial, Technique, Brawling, Basics, Psychology, Believability
**Charisma/Promo:** Charisma, Fluency

## HYBRID SYSTEM (FINALIZED)

### Structure:
- **Primary Archetype** - Defines match engine defaults
- **Secondary Archetype(s)** - Modify match behaviour/fan expectations
- **Hybrid Subtype** - Specific subtype from another archetype
- **Weighting** - Light or Heavy secondary influence

### Examples:
- Chris Benoit (prime): Fighting Spirit (Crashing Avalanche) + Junior Puroresu (Junior Bruiser)
- Dynamite Kid (prime): Junior Puroresu (Tiger Style Junior) + Fighting Spirit (Shooter Spirit)
- Randy Orton (early): Showman (Calculated Predator) + Heel Ace (Arrogant Monarch-lite)
- Eddie Guerrero (peak): Technical Showman + Junior Puroresu (Lucharesu Hybrid)

### Hybrid Tags:
- Hybrid Warrior
- Crossover Specialist
- Late Bloomer
- Style Shifter

### Career Evolution Examples:
- Bryan Danielson: Junior Puroresu → Fighting Spirit → King's Road touches → Showman elements
- Kenny Omega: Junior Puroresu → Showman (Mastermind/Spectacle Warrior hybrid)
- AJ Styles: Junior Puroresu (early) → Technical Showman (mid) → Fighting Spirit (late)
